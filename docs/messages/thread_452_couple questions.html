If I replace the logbook, I take it with me to lose under piles of
papers and books somewhere. =) If someone else replaces a logbook
for me (usually because I'm not around to do it myself), I let them
keep the logbook for themselves as thanks for taking care of the box.

I'm not a collector of logbooks, but I don't leave old ones in the
wild either.

Happy trails!
