From: grumpygrinchy (ffuselier@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-06-29 23:31:47 UTC
Frank and I took a drive out to the Delta Bay Club Resort this
afternoon. From Highway 12 go east through Rio Vista and pass the 160
turnoff. Soon, as you approach the Jackson Slough Road you will see a
red, white, and blue sign high above on the right that says:
<- This Exit
Delta Loop
10 Mile Scenic Drive

Turn right (south) onto Jackson Slough Road. Drive close to 2 miles
to a T stop. Turn left onto Brannan Island Road. Drive about 1 1/2
miles. Just past mile post #6 on your left will be the entrance to
the Delta Bay Club.

These are levee roads, 2 lanes on elevated roads surrounded by
farmlands. Enjoy the view and drive carefully!

See you all on July 9th.
