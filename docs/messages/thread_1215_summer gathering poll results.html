You might wonder why I didn't mention early August when outlining the
results. This is because I'm unavailable in early August and don't want
to plan an event I can't attend. Hope that's ok. :)

Mid to late August would be either the weekend of the 19th/20th or the
25th/26th. More people seemed to be available overall in mid-August but
I'd be willing to do another poll, or if you just want to post yes/no,
that's fine too.

The majority has spoken on location - Redwood Regional Park in Oakland.
Once we pick a weekend I'll look into reserving a table space of some kind
if possible.

Would it be safe to start encouraging those of you who said you'd plant to
start carving now? :)

People seem interested in a potluck. How do you envision this working, if
you are interested? Picnic style? Lunch or dinner? I'd be interested in
hearing more about this.

I'd like to settle on a date by next Wednesday. I'm planning on basing
the decision on what is said here over the next few days.

Thanks!

From: doublesaj@hughes.net (doublesaj@hughes.net) | Date: 2006-06-17 17:49:33 UTC

Eclipse:

Thanks for doing this! Gatherings are really fun and, I think, worth all the effort. You know you can get additional help from us too, just ask. (For starters, we'll bring a case of bottled water).

August 19 and 20 are highly desirable for us. Sunday the 27th is also doable. If it's the 26th we won't be able to make it : (

Pot luck: everyone bring something to share that can keep in the heat or have an individual ice source. Maybe a sign on your cooler that states "To Share" is in order so we're not shy of snooping in everyone's stuff.

Try to reserve a spot with the most shade you can get.

And, the very most important thing for a successful gathering: Think up a realy cool title for it!!
