Most successful weekend for us to date...

From: nicknameredinsf (lauracam1976@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-02-07 15:49:53 UTC

Hi everyone,

Redsf, SFmomo and Scott had our most successful LBing weekend.
Ususally we are not so
organized, but this weekend we found 13 boxes total and one HH (that
is good for us!).
Saturday we headed down towards Half Moon Bay. We stopped at "Where's
Waldo" but we
could not find him. Will have to email Tara and ask her about that
one. We then found
"Pagurus" in Moss Beach. We were the first LBing finders, however he
had been found by
others. One message inside the logbook read, "This is cool. What is
this?". He also wasn't
hidden at all, so we did our best to hide him. Next we did the Flying
Pigs two boxes along
the Purisima Creek trail. We were first finders there too. That was a
very nice hike. Then we
hit Anna & the Kid's Coastside Trail and found both of those boxes.
They had been found
earlier Sat. Sunday we went to Kennedy Grove Regional Park. We found
all 5 boxes in the
Boxer Family series. All were okay except Lucy Boxer had gotten
waterlogged and most
stamp images were washed away =/. We wrapped the book in some tissue
and hopefully
it will dry out more. We then found Tara's two Pirate boxes and ended
our day with the
"Itchy" box and picked up the Stanislaus HH. I forget whom to credit
with that HH. I'm
sorry. Will email the creator later today.
-Red
