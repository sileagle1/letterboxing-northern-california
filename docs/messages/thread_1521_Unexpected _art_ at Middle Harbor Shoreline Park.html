We had a little picnic yesterday at Middle Harbor Shoreline Park, and
saw the *oddest* thing.

The park had erected life-sized photo-blowups of coyotes, which we
imagine are intended to scare away the Canada Geese. The coyotes are
in the area where they are making the sand beach. (Right near a
letterbox of mine.)

At first, I thought I was looking at a particularly upset schnauzer
dog. But then I realized it was a photo, mounted on cardboard.

Weird.
