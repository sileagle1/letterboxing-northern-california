Gwen & I found a really cool one last July. So cool it still had ice crystals deep down inside the fault. Yes, you are not supposed to climb down in the fault, but we couldn't resist going down a little ways. The crevice was fairly narrow, but wide enough you couldn't jump across. If you are in the Mammoth Lakes area you can find it in our Enigmas, Illusions, Opposites, and Oddities near Hwy 395 letterbox. The Pink Elephant is at Fault is the start of the series.
