Wine Country Challenge

From: jceg5540 (greendoc@sonic.net) | Date: 2005-01-02 23:58:13 UTC

Hi BugZ, welcome back from Louisianna, hope you had a not to horrible
airport experience.

the wine Country Challenge was something I initiated after theJuly gathering
in Golden Gate Park. a very informal attempt to get more boxes planted in
Sonoma COunty (so selfish of me really so I would not have to drive so far to
find boxes!) At the GGG there was a challenge to play off the theme "I left my
Heart in SF" Thus, Lost my dart, flower cart, stolen tart boxes in the park.

I got to thinking I cold start a similar thiing up north, playing off the whole wine
country tourism stuff (which gets really old if you live here actually). Thus the
boxes Twine Country, Wino Country, Brine Country and now Gray Pine
Country. The Drew Clan already had a Wine Country Series, and Lea has
done a box as well as PAul in SF (Sleepy Bacchus). Of course it does not
have to rhyme, or even be wine related.

There was also a suggestion by me to get some Dartmoor style boxes planted
at a british pub here and I started the ball rolling wiht Rooster Taurus and
Deers of West Dartmoor.

So that is the history, and if you are interested please add a box!

Cheers,
Mim
