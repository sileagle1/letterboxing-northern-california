Valentine Traveler

From: Kel Gennert (arttrekker@tech21.com) | Date: 2006-01-25 20:34:18 UTC-08:00
I will be bringing a Valentine-themed traveler with me to the workshop on Saturday. What the logbook wants from those who desire the stamp is a roughly 2x2 piece of paper with something artful on it--verbal or not--relating to love, that can be affixed in the book. The artful and the love are the key words here. It can be collage or finger paint or doctored photograph or whatever, but the logbook wants to be more than just a record of sig stamps...it told me it wants to be a scrapbook, and it doesn't really care if you think you are artistic, it knows you are creative and will please it.

Looking forward to Saturday,
