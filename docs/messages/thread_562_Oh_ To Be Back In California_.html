Oh, To Be Back In California!

From: grumpygrinchy (ffuselier@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-02-24 14:19:52 UTC

We have a few more days in New Hampshire, then will start our trek
back to God's country, boxing as we go.

New England is still very beautiful, in a white, cold manner, but it
is not very conducive to letterboxing in winter. Well, we did
manage to find 24 boxes one Saturday recently in Connecticut, but
then more snow arrived to freeze and hide everything around again!

We've noted all the new letterboxes placed recently in Northern
California. Way to go! We can hardly wait to get out there search
for your handiwork. Thanks in advance for your efforts.

We've been carving while staying out of the frozen elements, so
perhaps we can increase the total number of boxes there once we
return. Hoping we'll be able to get to the Old Sac gathering April
30th and meet some of you. We want to put faces to the trail names!
