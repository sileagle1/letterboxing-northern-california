Jay was the California Web Master when we joined letterboxing(he lives on the east coast). At that time you (as Planter) could not post / edit your clues the way you do now. The Web Master for your area took them off the chat list and they had control over them. Obviously it was a lot of work for those web masters. Now Jay being a transplanted Californian always entered the California clues very quickly, in fact supprisingly fast. If you peruse the Interview series that Mark Pepe has, then you can find Jay's interview. If you go to The Drew Clan's web site you can see an example of his carvings, and the transition he has made in his carving abilities, which are now pretty incredible. In all of my emails to him and his back to me, I can say it is a pleasure to know a good guy. Once in awhile when the topics on the main chat list get heated, he always comes in with some words of wisdom, or some soothing comments. He is definately a "Silver Back" in this game.
