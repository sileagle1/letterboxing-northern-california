Sometime last year the stamp in our Dammed Shasta Letterbox was
traded out for some trinkets by unknowing geoca$hers. After a year,
we finally had the opportunity to travel the 500+ miles to add a new
stamp and change out the logbook. Never in my wildest dreams did I
think I'd actually make money letterboxing--but in lieu of the stamp
this particular letterbox made me $0.50 and a couple of beautiful
polished glass nuggets. ;) Still not sure how they crammed so much
stuff in this tiny, microletterbox?

Apparently, it's in the path of geoca$hers; so, we've found a new,
more distant home for it. Please ensure you have the new clues
before you head out for this one. It's basically a drive-by on I-5
(with a 10 mile roundtrip detour).

Dammed Shasta
Siskiyou County, CA
