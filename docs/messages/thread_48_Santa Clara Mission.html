Santa Clara Mission

From: sierrasally64 (Sierra64@aol.com) | Date: 2004-01-12 17:24:12 UTC
I forgot to have this one announced.

Placed by: Sierra Sally
Placement date: Jan 7 2004
State: California
County: Santa Clara
Nearest city: Santa Clara
Number of boxes: 1

Clues
Founded in 1777 Santa Clara Mission is the 8th California Mission to
be built. The prestigious Santa Clara University was founded here in
1861 at this mission and surrounds it. Santa Clara University was
the first college in California.

Difficultly Level: Easy, flat
Bring a compass - can be done without, but it's easier if you have
one.

Go to the front of Santa Clara Mission.

After visitng the inside come out the front door

Walk across the road to the cross in the traffic circle

Standing in front of the cross, walk 25 degrees toward the museum
building. This would be in NNE direction

Stop and read the two interpretive signs that face the mission

Turn your back on the signs and Look Left 345 degrees, NNW. See the
evergreen tree overhanging the lawn at the corner of the building?

Walk to the tree just to the right of that tree

Go around the left of the tree in a clockwise manner, the box is
wedged in the branches of the tree in the back about shoulder
height.

PLEASE be careful to wedge this box in the tree very well so that it
doesn't fall out of the tree and get discovered.



Before you set out, please read the waiver of responsibility and
disclaimer.
