Auburn area boxing....

From: Joseph (letterboxspector@hotmail.com) | Date: 2004-08-08 22:16:16 UTC
Wow! A very hot day of boxing. Hot, but productive. The
Letterboxspector family, with Special K, went after four boxes. Having
heard of the trouble finding the "Wide Window" box by Lemony Snicket
we allowed only three hours to look for this box. Unfortunately we
found this box and all the misery that goes with it. Next off to old
town Auburn. So glad to see the Old Auburn Letterbox back. We hunted
this one several times, with no luck. I wonder why? While we were
there we had to get the North Fork Dry Diggins. This log book is
nearly full. Last on our hit list is the elusive I-80 series box #1.
We have been one step behind Doublesaj and Old blue on this one many
times. But today we got it! This log book is nearly full also.

O.K. The kids, "On the hunt", "Box hopper", and "Special K" all want
to go swimming. I guess we will go home now. Thanks for all the boxing
fun today!
