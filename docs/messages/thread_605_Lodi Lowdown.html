Hi all,
They moved my office at work yesterday, so I took the day off, headed
south, & had a lot of fun getting most of the Lodi & Stockton area
boxes. It was the 1st time had gone out to solely hunt boxes instead of
picking them up as I was on the way to somewhere. Here's what I got:

Calaveras River Trail #1 & 2 (Watch the rocky banks, I fell flat on my
butt walking down to box #1.)
Alien Smorgasbord (Made myself sick sampling the junk food to make sure
the aliens hadn't tainted it.)
Spencer's Bone (So much duff, I looked all around the box & didn't see
it for 10 minutes, almost gave up.)
Able Was I Ere I Saw Elba (FF)
Lakeside Lovelies #1 & 2 (Got to use my new compass for the 1st time.)
Lodi Little Lamplight (Would have like to be there later to see the
lamps on.)
Lodi Libations (Grapes weren't getting crushed by wine press well
enough, so I left an little extra weight in the box to help out.)

I tried for the Basic Bunny, but one of the park maintenence guys came
driving up in his golf cart just as I was about to reach for where I
thought it was & asked if I needed any help. After that, they
suspiciously watched me from the maintenence yard as I wandered around
trying to look nonchalant. I finally gave up & moved on. It'll
definately be a great place to go back to with my daughter. I also
tried for the River Retreats, but I'm pretty sure #1 has gone missing, &
it was getting late, so I decided not to try for #2. I'll send more
detailed reports out the box planters shortly. I also got to play with
my new colored stamp pads & had a lot of fun making the images more
interesting. So, thanks to Grumpy Grinch, mtnbikemanns, beadgirlbecky &
Da Kool Kats for putting all those boxes out there.
