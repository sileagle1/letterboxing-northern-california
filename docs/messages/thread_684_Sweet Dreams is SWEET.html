How wonderful...Tuesday morning is the day I allow myself a few hours
hiking time and there is a new series in Petaluma. I hightailed to
Putnam Regional Park and got the set. Very nice! And wonderful
wildflowers this time of year. Moon Rabbit did an excellent job.
Allow extra time as this seems to be a very popular park with lots of
traffic....bring binoculars or field guide books....I found making it
look like I was "botanizing" a good cover. I did key out a new
wildflower for me (yello parentucellia) so not far from the truth.
