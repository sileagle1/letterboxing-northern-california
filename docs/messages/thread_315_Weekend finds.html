Weekend finds

From: deiselluver (deiselluver@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-11-01 05:15:31 UTC


Hi to all. Today Jeff and I set out to do some LBing. We started
with a Hybrid. Letter to Garcia in Peteluma because we were on the
way to Healdsburg for Healdsburg Quartet. Well we DNF Garcia. A dead
bird in the grass that made us leave though. WE did however find all
4 of the Quartet. From there we went to do a few Geocaches. 1 was
about 3 miles on Healdsburg Ave. That one was destroyed by someone
or something. WE found items all over. :(. Then we went on to do
another Cache in Windsor. Found that one. So we found 5 out of 7.
Not bad for a days work. Since we only have a total of 9 Letterboxes
found we can honestly say that todays #1 Quartet is our favorite one
so far. WE had lunch after we found it in this cute little deli and
the memorabilia was just wonderful. Brings back memories of my
younger days... LOL.All in all we had a good day. A little sore from
using the muscles we don't use all week but we had a good time. Still
wating for Vertigo 1 to resurface so we can do 1 and 2. Who knows
and maybe #3??????????
