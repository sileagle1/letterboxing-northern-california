Speaking of HH's, I'd like to make a little comment: when you drop one
off, please be sure to stamp the box stamp into the HH's log.
Theoretically the HH logbook should have a record of all the boxes
visited by the HH and every person who carried it. Please don't make
the picker-upper have to stamp the box's stamp into the log!
Invariably they will be in a hurry and just grab the HH, and then find
out when they get home that the box stamp was not done (which suppose
would mean they should return to the box and get it. Like that's gonna
happen).
