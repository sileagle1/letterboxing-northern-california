From: Inbal Kashtan (inbalka@mindspring.com) | Date: 2004-11-05 15:21:00 UTC-08:00
Hurray! Hurray! We just returned from this hike, and we loved the whole
experience! Truly lovely hike and stamps, and wonderful hiding spots. We
forgot to bring colors, unfortunately, so just had our three-color stamping
pad - a poor substitute.

We were the first at this one, found Blackbird's HH and hid one we had found
at Fairy Ring (also planted by Blackbird, and also very sweet).

Do pay attention to the well-written clues. At one point we thought
Blackbird had made a mistake in identifying the material something was made
of, and scrambled up a difficult non-path for no reason whatsoever....

Also, we picked up the regular trail map instead of the self-guided tour -
both are in the same map box, so pay attention to this, too.

Thank you so much, Blackbird! We can't wait for more (any coming???!). And
we got re-inspired to create another series for the East Bay. Hopefully
soon.
