Box Status Report

From: Lea Shangraw Fox (princesslea@alamedanet.net) | Date: 2004-01-04 20:50:01 UTC
I've been meaning to do an update of the boxing I managed to squeeze in during the
holidays. I did finally get over the 200 mark, with a lot of help for the Googled group
down in San Luis Obispo. Just before Christmas I found the following boxes which
are alive and well:

Buzzard's Halloween Letterbox
Magiz the Crow
Bella of Brockhall
Log a Log
The Other Building

These were all in SLO.

Next, right after the holidays we went to Tahoe, and on the way I picked up In-N-Out
Burger in Davis and I-80 series #1 in Auburn. Unfortunately the rest of the series and
all the other boxes in Tahoe were under the snow.

Did everyone see that there are 2 new boxes planted on the peninsula (Palo Alto and
Mountain View)? I'm so glad, because that area needs some coverage.

-- Princess Lea
