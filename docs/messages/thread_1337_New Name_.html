From: Lea Shangraw Fox (princesslea@alamedanet.net) | Date: 2006-09-02 18:16:55 UTC
Bethany explained to me that DVN2R CKR is the "family" name, and the stamp they used
when her kids were small to represent that the whole group had found the box. Now that
her kids are older and more capable of stamping in their own identities (RNRB=Runner Bee
and TRKR=Trekker), mom decided she needed a separate stamp and so now she's
Daelphinus, which is some sort of astral dolphin. There's also a dad (TRKEE=Trekkie),
although he is often overseas, but I had the opportunity to meet him in Portland at the
Sam Hill gathering. That's a very elusive exchange to have and I am very proud to have
nabbed it.

-- Princess Lea (who will never change her trail name)
