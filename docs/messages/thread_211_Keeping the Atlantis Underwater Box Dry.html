Keeping the Atlantis Underwater Box Dry

From: paulonthepark (pgonyea@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-08-09 00:09:23 UTC
A friendly reminder for those who plan on hunting down the
underwater letterbox, "The Hunt for the Temple of Neptune of the
Lost City of Atlantis"...

Please take a little extra care to help keep the insides of the box
dry. Bring some paper towels with you. When you pull the box up out
of the water, there's likely to be a small amout of dirt or algae on
it, even though its not in stagnant water. Dry off the outsides
before opening the box. When closing the box, check to see that
there is no dirt, sand or grit in the watertight seal. Also make
sure that the plastic bags for the journal and stamp don't get in
the way of the seal as well.

When I last checked, a bit of dirt broke the seal on the box,
letting in a small amount of water -- enough to soak the journal,
which was in a plastic ziploc that wasn't completely zipped. Until I
replace the journal, please feel free to stamp your journal w/o
stamping in the box's journal.

Thanks!

Paul in SF
