From: black_cavalier_1642 (Nils.A.Hedglin@Intel.Com) | Date: 2005-02-21 03:42:13 UTC

I scoped out hiding places today for the Sacramento Gathering &
wanted to warn people about the parking cost. The parking garage fee
is between $1.25 & $1 per 30 minutes depending on how long you are
there. Once you go over 6 hours, you will hit the maximum daily fee
of $13. There is meter parking on the street, but I think the cost
is a quarter per 15 minutes & there's a 2 hour maximum before you
have to move your car. The meters only take quarters or a debit
card. The garage takes cash up to $20 bills & a debit card.
