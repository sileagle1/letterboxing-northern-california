From: paulonthepark (pgonyea@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-05-22 01:15:13 UTC
Since I had my best friend visiting me from Key West I just HAD to
introduce her to letterboxing. So we hunted down Princess
Lea's "Where the Buffalo Roam" and we planted this new box, called
Sunbird. Of course, she's now indoctrinated.

The Sunbird box sits in a location with gorgeous sweeping views of
San Francisco. It's waiting for you. Be sure to wear your strong
vision eyeglasses when you go to look at the clues. [EVIL GRIN]

Also, it's been reported (and now confirmed) that my new "Yes, Noe"
box is missing. Someone appreciated a thing of beauty and took it
for themselves... but what goes around, comes around. I'll be
replacing it very soon. (Thanks for the report of my vanished box,
Amanda from Seattle!)

I've been recruiting new letterboxers, and I'm now hearing that a
number of folks are ready to jump in! San Francisco will soon have
doubled in its number of letterboxes... and we'll keep plantin' and
huntin'!
