Gung Hay Fat Chow! Today I planted the next in my Lunar New Year's
series - Year of the Ox. It won't appear in the list of new boxes on
AQ since it is piggy backed onto the old clue. I still remember the
day six years ago when I was sitting around, chomping at the bit to
find a letterbox in my area (there were none to be found that I
hadn't already gotten) and decided, what the heck, I'll just take all
this LB energy and plant one myself. It's been a challenge keeping
this series alive and well in an urban setting, but I have definitely
enjoyed adding to the series every year (it's good to have deadlines!)
