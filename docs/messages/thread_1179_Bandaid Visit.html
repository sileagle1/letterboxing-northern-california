From: Eclipse (eclipse@tuliphead.com) | Date: 2006-05-22 10:26:41 UTC-07:00
Hi all,

I used to live in that part of town (the Tenderloin, aka the 'loin), and
one of my favorite restaurants over there is Naan N Curry, on Eddy at
Leavenworth:
http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&hl=en&q=398+eddy,+san+francisco,+ca&om=1

The food is Pakistani/Indian, very tasty, and very affordable. It's a
spacious restaurant, and the noise level isn't deafening. You order at
the counter, sit, and wait for your food to be brought to you. Beverages
(other than lassis) and tableware are all self-serve, to help cut down on
price. The menu can be seen here:
http://www.naanncurry.com/
(IIRC it is right on the corner at Leavenworth, so ignore the site's
mentioning of "Levenworth & Jones".)

Another good restaurant in that area is Golden Era Vegetarian Restaurant,
a Chinese/Vietnamese/Pan-Asian restaurant (run by Vietnamese devotees of a
Vietnamese Buddhist lama, if I recall correctly). It's at O'Farrell and
Leavenworth. The food is fantastic, and completely vegetarian/vegan; they
serve lots of fake meat dishes that are so close to the real thing you'll
be hard pressed to tell the difference. This is more expensive than NNC
but again, the food is absolutely great. Menus here:
http://www.goldeneravegetarian.com/
Map:
http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&hl=en&q=572+Ofarrell+St,+San+Francisco,+CA+94102

If it's Friday or Monday I can join you all - Saturday and Sunday I'll be
in Sac.

If Indian or Asian won't work, let me know - I know of a number of other
good restaurants in that area.

Enjoy!

Eclipse
