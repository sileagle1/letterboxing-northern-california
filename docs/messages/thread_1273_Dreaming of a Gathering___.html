From: Lisa Lazar (lazar.bauer@earthlink.net) | Date: 2006-07-25 14:52:09 UTC
Instead of creating great letterboxes, I've merely been dreming about
our upcoming gathering.

I think you would all have found this pretty amusing....

The lettreboxers I invented, as well as the locations...

The long, lanky gentleman with the astonishingly long legs, dressed in
black jeans, a white Edwardian dress shirt and an Edwardian hair
cut...The other gentleman who refused to take off his rubber werewolf
mask....Both were claiming to be OB Juan, of course.

The hitherto undiscovered limestone cave systems that Sleepy Cat and I
found zillions of letterboxes in.

And the fact that the whole dream has a particular Nick Cave song as
its soundtrack. (The one where he sings about "the shores of Lake
Tahoooo" which may be a reference to ArtTrekker.)

This is what you get when I write messages prior to making coffee.

Cheers!
Lisascenic
