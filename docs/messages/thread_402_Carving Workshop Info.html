Hey all! If you are planning on attending the event, here are a
few reminders...

It should run from 1-5, the first hour probably being a bit of meet
and greet (I don't know any of you!), some people are bringing
snacks or drinks (if you've asked me about it already, I'm thinking
you are going to do it) I'll have paper plates and cups.

sketches, burning questions...it would be good to bring a piece
of your favorite carving medium as well. I'll have some
Safe-T-Cut for folks to play with. And I've got carving tools, but
feel free to bring your own. I've also got plenty of paper and
pencils for your sketches.

We'll see you then! And please remember NOT to park on the
side of the street across from the school, use the small lot in
front or park on the SCHOOL side of the street. Thanks!
