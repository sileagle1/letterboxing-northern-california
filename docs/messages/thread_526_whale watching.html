Hi Tonny,

We went with a company called Monterey Bay Whale Watch. They were
particularly recommended cause they have a real naturalist on board. Their
info is:

Monterey Bay Whale Watch
Monterey Bay Whale Watch offers quality, educationally oriented whale watch
trips led by Marine Biologists and Marine Naturalists. We are the number one
whale watch operation in Monterey Bay with our unique 3-hour trip that
offers an unrushed schedule allowing ample time to observe whales and time
to search for dolphins, porpoise, and killer whales.
831-375-4658. www.montereybaywhalewatch.com

There's a coupon to use their services and many other things on the Monterey
visitors bureau website (search for it).

I hope you enjoy!
