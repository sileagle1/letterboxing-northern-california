From: grumpygrinchy (ffuselier@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-04-23 06:25:55 UTC

We have discovered what we believe to be a NEW letterboxing letter
count: PF, which stands for premature find. We made a trek to Santa
Rosa today and were the pleased first finders on the first four of
Mimulus's Dudley's been kidnapped series. Fabulous stamps in a most
awesome environment. Combined with a good cardio workout, it was a
great series. Somehow we arrived at the final site to rescue Dudley
and the RCMP payroll, and his evil captor had not yet brought him to
the site! PF!! Here we came to save the day, only to be foiled
again! Zounds!!!
