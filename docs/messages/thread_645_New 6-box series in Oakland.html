Hello folks,

After months of plotting, First President and I have FINALLY completed "The
Greatest Liar" series. This was a joint effort - 3 of the stamps are his
quick carvings, three are my more painstaking (but don't expect much)
efforts. This is a very sweet walk in Oakland's Joaquin Miller park - but
follow the directions, it doesn't start where you might think.

Clues are just on LbNA - I just haven't made the time to join AtlasQuest
yet. (Okay, I know it won't take long, but I'm strapped for time, what can I
say? I also get daunted by how to list our now otherwise-named son, who is
likely to change his name when his coin hobby wanes anyway.)

There's also a math puzzle with mailed prizes, and itty bitty finders'
prizes set up by First President. I imagine the young ones would
particularly enjoy these. Note that there are finders' prizes for various
finders, not just first finders. (That's what the math puzzle is about.)

I hope you enjoy! Write and let us know.
