From: Lea Shangraw Fox (princesslea@alamedanet.net) | Date: 2007-01-07 22:51:58 UTC
It's always good to hear about new boxes that are planned for Oakland!
I am planning to do some box maintenance up in The Oakland Hills some
time this week, and nab the boxes I am missing up there too. I have
been in a carving funk too - I feel the need to out-do myself and this
leaves me frozen in terms of attempting anything. But I do need to
replant Sushi House and Playland, and also do the next box for the
Lunar New Year series. I am also going up to Tuolumne County with the
Girl Scouts next weekend and may or may not get a chance to do some
boxing then.

The good news is that I got to letterbox every single day from
Christmas through New Years Eve! My family was very patient (I guess
you could consider it a Christmas present that they tolerated all the
hunting). We were on a road trip down to San Diego to watch the Cal
Bears play in the Holiday Bowl (Go Bears! Missed you DoubleSaj& Old
Blue!). Went the I5 route on the way down, and Highway 99 back for
maximum box exposure. While most of the finds were down in So. Cal.,
in N. Cal. I did manage to finally nab some of Sierra Sally's Fresno
Cultural Trail boxes and the new ones in Hilmar.

It looks like I am not going to start any new jobs until the end of
January, so I am hoping to get some boxing done this month, weather
permitting. I need to hit Vacaville, Monterey, Marin, Petaluma....
Anyone wishing to play hooky and come out letterboxing one day, just
let me know!

-- Princess Lea
