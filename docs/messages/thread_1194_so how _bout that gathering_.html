Hi all,

As it turns out i still haven't had much time to think about this, but I
thought I'd get some ideas out before too much more time went by.

It looks to me like the main questions right now are:
- location
- date
- activities (?)

I'm all for taking this gathering somewhere new that isn't necessarily San
Francisco-centric. I'm definitely in favor of taking it somewhere that
allows the maximum number of people to participate, factoring in distance,
weather, and ease of use for all interested participants. To that end,
I'd suggest that Lisa's suggestion of the Redwood Regional Park in Oakland
looks like it fits the bill, with lots of hiking trails, picnic tables
with disabled-accessible areas, closer than SF (and no SF traffic!) for
folks outside the Bay Area, but still in the BA so likely to be warm but
not too warm. We can reserve a picnic area if we choose, so we could
start or top the day off with a potluck or picnic.

If this doesn't appeal to anyone, other suggestions are welcome!

As far as the date goes, I'd personally lean towards the end of August,
mainly because I'll be away in early August and would like to at least
have a weekend back in town before the gathering to finish preparations on
any boxes I might make and actually go plant them. I am of course open to
other dates - end of July or early September even, but end of August, the
26th or 27th, seems like a good target to me.

Considering activities, of course I assume folks will want to plant boxes
and spend much of the time looking for boxes. It being summer and the
season of barbeques, would there be any interest in a potluck, picnic, or
barbeque of some kind? It would be a nice way to cap off a day and set
some time aside for stamp exchanges.

I know many of you have coordinated events in the past, so I urge you to
please make suggestions, speak up, and generally let me know of your ideas
and tips. I'm perfectly happy to co-coordinate with anyone who wants to
pitch in... I mainly wanted to get the ball rolling and help this happen.
:)

Looking forward to hearing from you all -

Eclipse
