We found Birthday Bear in the Sacramento City Cemetery on January 1st and
just found a letterbox large enough to accommodate it last Sunday (9th). We
planted it in a box we found "somewhere in Sacramento". So it's alive and well. I
really can't remember how many people had signed in but if you're really
interested, contact me off list and I'll tell you which box I planted it in and you
can check on it. However, I already posted to the list that I planted this HH
in a box "Somewhere in Sac" so someone might have gotten it already.

Also, when you plant a hitchhiker, how do you change out the book if you
never know where it is? Do you put something in the book that asks people to
contact you if it is full? If it is full, how do you replace it if it isn't
near you? Do people ever see their hitchhikers again? Let me know your
experiences. Thank you.
If you want a HH back, post a note on the main board (LbNA) with "HH (name)
called in" in the subject line. I see that all the time. Also, often, if
someone finds a full log book, HH or regular Lb, they will notify the planter and
tell them about it. We had that happen and we asked the person to mail it back
to us. We let him keep the box (to use to plant his next box) as a 'thank you'
and so postage would be cheaper.
