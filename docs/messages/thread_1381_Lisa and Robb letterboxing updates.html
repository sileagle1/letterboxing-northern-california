From: Lisa Lazar (lazar.bauer@earthlink.net) | Date: 2006-10-18 15:19:53 UTC
This past weekend, Robb and I checked up on a few of our
letterboxes. So here's a little update:

Robb replaced the Bay Trail Letterbox, which is part of the East Bay
Trail series in San Leandro.

We also checked in on Icons for California: San Leandro. Despite
suggestions that this letterbox had gone astray, I am happy to
report that it is safe and snug.

Finally, Kathy from Team Tysonosaurus sent me the following note
regarding the Floral Depot in Oakland:

"...that stretch of sidewalk is completely fenced off right now due
to construction. It looks like the gate to which your letterbox is
stuck is just fine, but no one can get to it without climbing an 8
foot chain link fence."

Dem XX Men tell me that the sign that was holding the Macro Economics
letterbox in the Micro/Macro series in Fremont has been uprooted.
They sent along photos, and I'm sorry to say that I think they're
correct.

You can read about our "bike rally" on the Bay Trail at:
http://howsrobb.blogspot.com/
