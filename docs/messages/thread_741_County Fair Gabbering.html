Well, I guess it is official because I entered it on the atlasquest
event planner. See the below link to RSVP. I went with county fair,
but only because the Sonoma County Fair starts a few days later.

It is not important to have a theme really, but the whole Gold Rush
thing was so excellent, and Left my Heart in SF for GGG the year
before as well, so, why not? I propose any boxes become some sort
of traveling event troupe ala Boch's circus. but it is not
necessary. I will carve and plant one, hopefully Wascally and
MoonRabbit will consider adding one apiece and if anyone from father
away wants to entrust me in finding a hiding place in my
neighborhood you can mail me a box before hand or just hide it that
morning. OF course, not too many boxes since we want to have time
to talk...

At this point I will commit to providing various sausages, buns,
condiments, grilled peppers and onions. Ice, iced tea and coffee.

I think Carole could be persuaded to bring some of her great baked
goods. And as long as everyone else doesn't decide to bring chips
and salsa we will be in great shape. (you know ....the potluck
where everyone brings bread, chips and nothing to drink!)
