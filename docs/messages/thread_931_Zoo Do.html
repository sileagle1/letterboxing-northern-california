From: Hedglin, Nils A (Nils.A.Hedglin@Intel.Com) | Date: 2005-10-24 13:38:00 UTC-07:00
I was excited to see that artTrekker had dropped her extra Hawaii boxes
off at the Sacramento zoo. I am off today (Monday) & had been wanting
to take my daughter to the zoo for a while. I was there by 9:30, hoping
to beat Da Kool Kats & DoubleSaj & Old Blue since I figured they'd be
racing to get to the boxes too. My daughter didn't seem to interested
in the sedentary animals, but loved anything that was awake & moving
(red pandas, orang-utans, etc). Unfortunate, my daughter's fear of
sirens kicked in when the gibbons started howling, but we did have fun.
It took me a while to get the Aloha Box (bird quiz clue), because I
missed one of the clue birds since it was tucked away around a corner.
But, I got to it & was glad to see we were the 1st to stamp in. I was
surprised how fast spiders spun their webs around the box in just 2
days. The Island Magic box was much tougher ince I couldn't find the
key landmark the clue referred to. I wandered around the zoo 3 times &
couldn't find it. Finally, it was getting passed my daughter's nap time
so I gave up & decided to leave. At that moment, I finally saw the
landmark. The box is tough to get to though, because you really have to
get down & reach it at a weird angle. So I used my daughter's stroller
to try to block sight of the hiding place. As I was reaching for the
box, a man walked up behind me & stared talking to me. I thought "Oh
great, I've just blown this box to the zoo staff." But then I realized
the guy was saying "Sorry Black Cavalier, you're not getting First
Finder on that one." I stood up & turned around expecting to see Gary
or Old Blue. But it was neither, & the lady with him was calling me by
my real name too. They finally introduced themselves & I realized I
could finally confirm the existence of the elusive Grumpy Grinch. They
kindly provided cover for me to finally get the box & then left since
they were on their way out. So, thanks aT for giving me a good excuse
to get my daughter to the zoo & the lucky occurance of meeting Grumpy
Grinch. It's the 1st time I've met a boxer "in the field".

Black Cavalier
