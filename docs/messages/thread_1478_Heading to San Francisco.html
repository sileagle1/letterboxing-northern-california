Re: [LbNCA] Heading to San Francisco

From: laura camara (lauracam1976@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-03-06 09:01:28 UTC-08:00
There aren't many (if any) boxes near where you are staying, but I'd head to Golden Gate park by taking the 38 Geary and getting off at Arguello or Land's End/Sutro baths area by taking the 38 to 48th ave. I don't know how long you have, but this would allow you to find several boxes on one trip.

Where you are staying is not really close to Moscone Cntr, fyi. You'll be within walking distance of Japantown and VanNess Muni, but if you will be needing to get to Moscone Cntr, you'll have a good walk through a not so great area or you'll need to take the bus.
-red in sf
