2 weeks = P1F11

From: black_cavalier_1642 (Nils.A.Hedglin@Intel.Com) | Date: 2005-01-12 21:24:14 UTC

I crossed the 10 Found mark at lunch today. I picked up the Folsom
Prison & Folsom Powerhouse boxes, both I'd highly recommend.
Compasses helpful, but not required.

I had to walk through a contstruction site to get to the Powerhouse
box, but since the construction was actually in another area, the
workers didn't seem to care. It was fun to read the log book since
it's been around for so long. I saw Doublesaj's entry saying it was
the 2nd box they'd found. There were a lot of other 1st & 2nd timers
that entered in the log.

The Folsom Prison box has a beautiful stamp & an easy drive-by. Just
watch out for the chickens.
