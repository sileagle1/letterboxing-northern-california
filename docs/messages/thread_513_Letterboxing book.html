Re: Letterboxing book

From: rscarpen (letterboxing@atlasquest.com) | Date: 2005-02-10 19:17:24 UTC

> Yes, got the wrong Ryan.

*hehe* You both got the wrong Ryan. I think the book you're talking
about was written by Randy Hall. But I'm honored to think someone
would think I could write such a book, however. =)

As for being redundant or not--it's pretty much the same material
you'll find on the websites. For an experienced letterboxer, there's
not much you'll learn from it--though I've heard rumors that Randy has
some real live clues hidden in it. Not that I've bothered to figure
out what they might be since he lives in Pennsylvania, or even know if
the rumors really are true or not. *shrug*

It's nice to have if you want to explain letterboxing to someone
without the use of websites, however.

Happy trails!

-- Ryan
