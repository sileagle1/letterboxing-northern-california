NCA - Davis box updates!

From: Pete (jiggs111@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-05-24 01:19:58 UTC
Hi all!

We took a short trip out to Davis today, to try and pick up a box
or two, and update the Best of Davis series. Here's what we found...

A Rocky Trail - Missing (?) we spent about 90 minutes looking for
what was described as a 10 minute "kid-friendly" box...with nothing
to show for it. According to the clues, the box was near a white emu
egg shaped rock and a loaf of french bread!! Problem is the whole
dry stream bed is full of rocks that could fit the bill! The kids
got bored after about 15 minutes of searching, so they enjoyed the
nearby playground! I finally gave in and we moved on to...

Best of Davis 1-4

We had done this series last summer, using a store-bought stamp, so
figured we'd retrace and restamp as we went. This would also give us
an opportunity to pick up the replanted #3 as a "find".

Box #1 - alive and well. Book is absolutely full. We didn't have a
bok that small, otherwise I would have replaced it.

Box #2 - alive and well, with a replaced book already there.

Box #3 - box and book are there, but some little "sweetheart" has
stolen the stamp. According to the clues, the stamp was replaced
only a week or two ago. The previous visitors on 8 May stated the
stamp was missing at that time.

Box #4 - alive and well. Book is getting pretty full.

Pete aka Jiggs
