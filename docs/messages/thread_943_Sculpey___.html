If anyone is using Fimo or Sculpey with any regularity, I would highly
recommend buying a toaster oven, and reserving that oven for craft use
only.

It turns out that these types of clays release dangerous vapors that
re-condense and settle in your oven, and can be ingested by you or
your family. Don't take chances, with this or othher art materials.
