AND the destination! ;) If the destination involves meeting other

people!!!

Hey, I just found out I have to go to Southern Louisiana for a week
in a few months--and I even considered driving the 2,700 miles one
way for it just so we could letterbox along the way (imagine all the
cool places we could visit along the way!!!). After realizing it
would take over 5 days driving 500 miles each (without deviating for
letterboxes along the way) just to get there (with 2 young kids in

there instead. If I had all the time in the world, you'd better
believe I'd have driven it. So, yes, this hobby has certainly
changed our mindset--is it dedication? Nah, probably more like
mental issues... ;)

Hoping we will be able to juggle our schedule & finances so we can

letterboxes in the process... ;) Hope to see you then,

From: Gwen and Don Jackson (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-03-06 13:57:23 UTC-08:00
Gwen & I are on a letterboxing junket in SLO area and we are finding it difficult to locate phone plug ins. If this message gets out before 3/9 then we found one. For info regarding the Kernville gathering in April you can go to Silent Dougs web site and most of your questions will be answered. A 9 year old should be able to go rafting BUT you would have to rent an appropriate PFD (life jacket) from one of the outdoor stores in town as we do not have any that small. A 5 1/2 tear old would be pretty iffy. and would make the guide extremely nervous. A 2 1/2 year old would not be able to go rafting but frazzeled mothers would be okay. Several camping areas are listed in the info sheet that is posted on Silent Dougs web site. When we get back to Kernville we will be able to handle you questions.
Don
