No Foolin' Letterboxing Gathering: Sunday April 1, 2007 Alum Rock Park, San Jose

From: reneepaigehartsook (reneepaigehartsook@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-12-06 22:15:14 UTC
Hi Folks!

Princess Lea suggested I post the announcement for this gathering
here too to make sure everyone hears about it.

We have a listing on Atlas Quest for this gathering, but am happy to
send info directly to anyone interested.

Basically, we're putting together something in Alum Rock Park, San
Jose, CA for April Fool's Day. We'll rent one or more group picnic
areas, bring food, and try to recruit someone to give lessons on
stamp carving or other interesting topics. If folks are bringing
small kids, we are happy to plan some activities specific for the
little ones (our kids are teenagers, but we remember what it is like
to have little ones).

Any requests, please contact me. Also, please please please RSVP.
Parking is decent at Alum Rock, as the date approaches there will be
some messages going out on the logistics to make sure everyone has a
hassle-free time. Plus, we want to have enough grub for everyone!

Thanks,
Renee
