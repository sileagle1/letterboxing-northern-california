Group Photo from Sherlock Holmes Gathering

From: doublesaj12 (doublesaj@hughes.net) | Date: 2006-10-08 23:45:24 UTC
Hi All:

I've posted the group photo I took at the wonderfully fun gathering
yesterday. We don't know why but this gathering was just perfect!
Was it the great letterboxes, the free, lack-of-structure feel it
had or the great combination of letterboxers themselves that made it
so special? Even the fact that we had a group of 24 hanging around
at the end of the day to grab this great photo was special! Old Blue
and I just had a ball! Take a look at the group shot and see if I
got the names of everyone correct (not my strong suit!)

Back row from left: Doublesaj & Old Blue, RedSF, Boo's Clues, Celtic
Cutie, In Cahoots, Green Tortuga, Da Kook Kats (Carol & Gary),
ArtTrekker, Tysonasaurus, Robb, Lisascenic,
Front row: SFMomo, Tako Bunny, 3Vix, Spygirl, Heather, Baby
Bookworm, Dem XX Men (Hunter and Kara) Baby Ryan, Starsing, Alaska
HomeSchoolMommy.

A few folks had to leave early including Grumpy Grinch and Red Hat
Queen (AK HSM's mom!)

Thanks to AK HomeSchool Mommy for getting us all together, making
for a very fun event indeed!
