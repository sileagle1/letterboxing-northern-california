Got 5 boxes today and one HH, thought we'd let you all know.

Sobrante Ridge by pawca in El Sobrante, a beautiful, easy shady,
hike. We were first finders!
Yo, Ho, Yo HO and A Pirate's Life for Me by Tara She's a great carver
and these stamps can be found very close to the aforementioned
Sobrante box. Also in this wonderful gem of a regional park (Kennedy
Grove, off San Pablo Dam Road) are at least 8 other boxes. This place
is a real treat for all ages.
At Point Pinole Letterboxes in Richmond we got #1 but had to be
at "the in-laws" by a certain time and regretfully had to postpone
the other 2 boxes here until another day.
Sandlot in Benicia was our 3rd try. First time it was too dark,
second time we didn't have our book with us but this time we got it.
Not that it's difficult to find. the tough part is waiting for a time
when no one's looking!

Placed the Cupid Stamp HH in one of these boxes. This HH is from
Connecticut and we've had it quite a while because there's not been a
box large enough to fit it in. So, go out and get it!
