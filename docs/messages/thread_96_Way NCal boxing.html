Hi all-

We had a great visit with the folks in Redding, and did some
boxing "damage" while there. To wit:

- Added both the Ide Adobe and FunHog Lake Plateau stamps to our
logbook collection. I hadn't been to the Ide Adobe since I was a
schoolkid, and it's changed a lot! What a terrific hiding place.
(Anyone attempting FunHog Lake Plateau can earn extra brownie points
if they find the camera case Mom appears to have dropped...) FunHog,
the Plateau box is soon going to run out of pages, after seeing more
traffic than the Adobe.

- Spent a day out Whiskeytown way, and hiked into Chrystal Creek.
The box itself appeared to be missing, but wow, what a beatiful spot.
Even my Mom, Maven of all things Shasta County, didn't know about
this place! (In all fairness, it's been closed many years because of
the work camp up the road, now closed.)

- Took the spare time freed up by someone else cooking to carve and
place two of my own about and not about town. Haven't been able to
get the LbNA site to cooperate in letting me post, so y'all may have
to wait a bit.

- Dammed Shasta is fine, and they've resumed tours at the dam again.
The tour is great on a hot day, as it's 65 degrees inside.

Celt et al.
