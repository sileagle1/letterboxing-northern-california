Point Reyes 1/15

From: Lea Shangraw Fox (princesslea@alamedanet.net) | Date: 2006-01-14 19:38:06 UTC
I can't remember if we let everyone on the list know, but we decided to change the Point
Reyes trip to Sunday (tomorrow) due to the forecast of rain today. So if there is anybody
interested at the last minute, we'll be at the visitor's center at 10:00 on the 15th.

Sleepy Cat and I went down to the Monterey peninsula yesterday as I was on a pilgrimage
to get Jay Drew's Wharf Cat stamp. We hit several of Mtnbikemann's boxes on the way and
achieved our goal up on Tortilla Flats of that amazing cat stamp. We moved on to Pacific
Grove and got so horribly lost (in a dog park!) that we ended up in Pebble Beach. Luckily
we realized we were very close to another LB so we nabbed that one before finally getting
back and persevering in getting Sporty's Play Day. That one should have been a drive by,
but I think it took us almost 1 1/2 hours. Duh.

Then we went to Carmel for Kat from Moab's cute little This Old Man Came Rolling Home
series. I would love to have gotten her other boxes in town but the sun was down and we
didn't bring out flashlight or the Kool Kats with us to help find boxes in the dark. So, I'll
have to come down another day - am glad to see the area growing so nicely. I was there
about 18 months ago and there were only a handful of boxes to find at the time. The only
ones I found missing were Free Flying and Wags & Tails.

-- Princess Lea
