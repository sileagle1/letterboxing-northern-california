Thank you GG for the nice words about our Tribal Falls Letterbox. It
was recently recarved and replaced. The first one disappeared. As
you know, this is not a box for the "faint of heart." It's an
adventure!
Perhaps, this would have been an ideal box to make a WOM only box. It
may have generated a few more visits this way.

It's interesting to note that 4 of the 6 finds and attempts were by
the "older" boxers.

We've just returned from 5 days and 6 nights in downtown BLYTHE. Yes,
Blythe! Actually, it wasn't too bad. Just very hot. They had a big
rain Monday night and Tuesday (very unusual). They got just about
half of their annual rainfall in that time period and then yesterday
was as humid as MS!

We placed a box while there, but I don't forsee it getting many
visits.

It's good to be home.
