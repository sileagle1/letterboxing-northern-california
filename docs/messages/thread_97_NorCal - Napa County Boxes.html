Clan Jiggs took advantage of the "lovely" Ontario-like spring wet
weather here in CA to add to out find total, by taking a little road
trip into the heart of the Napa valley!

We were fortunate enough to find the following boxes in almost-
perfect condition...

Napa...Not Wine - a great box in a stunning setting located
in...well' you'll just have to figure that out! Log book was about
half-full, but soggy, even inside 2 ziplocks.

Westwood Hills Regional Park series #1 and 2 - both boxes found
after another leisurely, uphill stroll. #1 has seen about twice the
traffic as #2. Interesting to note that DoublesAJ and Old Blue
haven't hit these ones yet!!!

By the way, we had lunch in a super brewpub in Napa called the
Silverad Brewing Company...where we took a beer(!) tour of the Napa
Valley for a very reasonable $3.50!!! Food was great, too!
