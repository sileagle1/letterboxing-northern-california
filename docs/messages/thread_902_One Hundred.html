It helped that when I started, I was really obsessed, and that Robb
was in a show that I wasn't interested in seeing more than once. I
had a stretch of weekends free to hunt letterboxes. The Gold Rush
Romp greatly added to my count. I'm still learning the Bay Area, so
our usual weekend exploring combines nicely with letterboxing.

I'm really not a "counter" so it is funny that the day I decided to
tally, I actually had hit an even hundred. I'm somewhat of a bird
watcher, but don't count that either.

The one thing I do count is deer, but only during the period of the
year that I'm working at Glimmerglass Opera. My dear friend and
co-worker, Sheila, and I have a silly deer-counting contest. She
takes it much further than I....and keeps three tallies: live, dead,
and stuffed. I can't figure out the fractions on the taxedermy. I've
"busted" her for counting hay bales and archery targets, though!

Lisa
