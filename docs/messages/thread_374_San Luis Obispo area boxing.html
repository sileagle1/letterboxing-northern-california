The first time I went there in June of '03, I got 28 boxes in 36
hours--including sleeping and eating. The next time about 10 more and this time, 8-10
again. Although some have been pulled (some of Sir Balthazar's) I would plan to
spend 2 days of just boxing. There are several long 2-5 mile hikes (Ryan
Carpenter's boxes) one of which is written in Spanish!! If you're up for those, add a
day. There are many more in Cambria, Arroyo Grande, Los Osos and Morro Bay,
all of which are within 20 miles of SLO.

Maybe, 3 days. . .
