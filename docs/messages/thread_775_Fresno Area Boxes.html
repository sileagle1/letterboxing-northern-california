Traveling from Davis to the Fresno area for a cousin's 50th
anniversary, we were happy to find all 9 of the boxes we tried for.
Thanks to Sierra Sally, Da Kool Kats and Grumpy Grinch for the great
boxes--to wit:

Alien Smorgasboard
Able Was I Ere I Saw Elba
Say Cheese (really great place to have lunch!)
Fresno Water Tower (so interesting!)
Tiger Tale (first finders)
Fresno County Cultural Trail boxes #1, 3, 5 and 8
(box #1 had a slimy, soggy log that was beyond fixing, I'm sorry to
say but the stamp was usable.) Too bad there are so many reported
missing on this series. The idea is a good one and the stamps are great!

Thanks to all!!
Oh, there's a ferocious HH planted in one of the aforementioned boxes!
