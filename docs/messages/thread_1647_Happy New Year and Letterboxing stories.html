Happy New Year Everybody,
Just got an amazing story about one of my letterboxes. Had to share
it. My box is called Blast off and it's a cartoon rocket stamp near
Kennedy Space Center's Astronaut Hall of Fame. Here's the message:
Message:
Hi there, Just wanted to let you know that we found your box on
December 20, 2007. And... WHILE I WAS STAMPING, my husband
says, "Joanne, look up!" and there, up in the sky, was a rocket! How
cool is that! Thanks for the memory!

Reminds me of the time I found a hitchhiker called "Julie's 40"
celebrating her 40th's birthday. I found it on my 40th birthday!

I'm sure some of you have similar stories...please share
Sierra Sally
