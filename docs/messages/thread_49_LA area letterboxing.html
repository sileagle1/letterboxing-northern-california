From: trentlaudes (trentlaudes@excite.com) | Date: 2004-01-20 05:03:28 UTC
Amanda,
I will be very upset if you visit Pasadena and do not find at least
one of my Halloween Series LBs. There are also several nice boxes in
Descanso Gardens, located in La Canada Flintridge, just a couple of
miles north of Pasadena, which California Bear has recently checked
on and updated, and a 7-Series planted by Leslie in Pasadena's Arroyo
Seco. These three series will net you at least 15 boxes within a few
miles of each other. You may remember me and California Bear at the
SLO Gathering. My stampbook proudly carries both your and Princess
Lea's Nancy Drewish X-change stamps (and Ryans, of course).
--Buzzard
