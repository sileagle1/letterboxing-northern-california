about the show
The world premiere of Passing Strange takes musical theatre on a
whole new trip. It's the heartfelt and hilarious story of a young
bohemian who charts a course for "the real" through sex, drugs and
rock and roll. Loaded with soulful lyrics and overflowing with
passion, the show takes us from black, middle-class America to
Amsterdam, Berlin and beyond on a journey towards personal and
artistic authenticity.
