Semi-virtual box for GRR

From: Hedglin, Nils A (Nils.A.Hedglin@Intel.Com) | Date: 2005-04-11 14:20:22 UTC-07:00
I've got an idea for a great site to have a letterbox for the Gold Rush
Romp. Unfortunately, it's relatively tightly controlled & I'm worried
about the box disappearing before the end of the day. OTOH, due to the
uniqueness of the location, the hunting process will be quite intriguing
& different. So, I'm thinking about making it like a physical virtual
box. You follow the clues through the site & the last clue gets you a
piece of info that you give to me at the event. Then you get to stamp
in the box that I'm carrying with me.

My concern is for those that solve the clues, but might have to leave
early or don't see me again at the GRR. How should I handle the box for
those people? I could send the person a sticker with the stamp on it
for their book & they could send me one with their personal stamp for
the box's book. Or I could send them the whole box, but that would get
expensive. Now that I describe it, I guess it sounds a lot like
Mimulus' Print the Legend box.

Mimulus, what are you planning for your box after the GRR?

Thanks for any thoughts,
