My husband told me that he wants to start letterboxing with me!! I
can't believe it, I've been trying to get him interested for MONTHS.

his boxes will be very "green turtle"-ish, in that he swears they
will all be either ten mile hikes or you'll have to swim to them. I
said to him "whatever you want as long as you start boxing with
me!". Now we're thinking up a trail name for him...for some reason
I'm having a tough time with it. I've alread thought of great names
for a few other people (who don't box yet :P thats the next step!),
but am having such a hard time with my husband. Yeah, and I really
haven't picked a trail name yet either. We can be the noname family
or something :P
Anyway, hopefully you'll see some more boxes and a new stamp out
there!

Funny you should post this--I was just perusing Tara's website for
clues for our upcoming 'rim to rim trip' through CA and saw a
picture of her husband. I instantly assumed he was a member of the
Corps! Now, some 45 minutes later somebody confirms my suspicion. ;)

I'd say hooah--but I'm not sure if the USMC uses that term, as
well? As for trail names, what about 'never quit' or something to
do with a unit motto/mascot? To be honest, that's how my sister A-
Bear (from Idaho) got her trailname. Years ago, her squad mascot
was grizzly bears. She's been a big fan of bears ever since.

Perhaps, we'll see a few of you on some trails in CA in a few days?
As it's taken me almost a half day just to consolidate clues for
just a handful of regions of CA--boy, the boxing scene down there
has changed rather exponentially since our last trip through the
Golden State. Back then, we could have printed the clues to every
box in the state in about 40 pages--now I'm pushing over 200 pages
and that's only 2 'regions' from the LBNA map of CA. I haven't
gotten anywhere close to touching the Bay Area yet! I think Kinko's
will be printing these clues for me this time around as my printer's
fritzed!

Take care,
