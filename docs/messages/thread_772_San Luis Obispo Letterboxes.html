the central coast. Here's a quick report on the boxes we found, and
didn't find. Most were dry and well. We'll send individual reports
to the placers where there are specific condition problems to be
noted. Thanks to all who contributed to our fun! If you haven't
been to the SLO area to do some hiking and "boxing", we'd encourage
you to do so! Grumpy Grinch
