What Motivation!

From: grumpygrinchy (ffuselier@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-12-05 00:56:12 UTC

We received the nicest email today from a Letterboxer in North
Carolina who grew up in Lodi. He is bringing his family back to
Lodi for the holidays. He said he just checked to see if there were
any letterboxes in the area, and wrote to thank us for placing some
around here. He wants to share the letterboxing experience with his
parents as well as his kids. Does it get any better than this? If
you have placed any boxes anywhere, consider yourselves thanked!

Grumpy Grinch
