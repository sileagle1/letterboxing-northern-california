babble." meanwhile trying to read and spy on what she has in her hand.

you're one of us!!! after much laughter and explaining the "secret
handshake" exchanging, logging and story telling, JJ of Kensington and
CBHB lifesaver and i were off to find a box together.
don'cha luv this hobby!!!!

ahoy..its not really a secret "handshake". in fact there was a whole
discussion on this very topic on the big list. its more of a silly
thing. if you suspect the person who is acting strangely/trying to be
nonchalant/pretending to take a picture/is SO very interested in a
mushroom/observing tree bark etc, you ask them. "Which way is North?"
Without hesitation and with complete and utter confidence,they point
straight up to the sky. Then you know you have met one of your own,
one of the tribe, takes one to know one, one addicted to hunting
tupperware, a kindred spirit, you have engaged a LETTERBOXER!!!! LOL
