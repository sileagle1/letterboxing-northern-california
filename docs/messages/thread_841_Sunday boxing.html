Sunday boxing

From: Kel Gennert (arttrekker@tech21.com) | Date: 2005-08-08 19:16:33 UTC-07:00
Well, I just couldn't stand it, I had to get outta Dodge for cooler climes & new boxes, so I acted on a wild hair and drove down to San Francisco for the day yesterday. Added the following to my book:

Barkin' at the Moon
Nepenthe--great location
Mim's Tin Tin series--awesome! (a little brain cramp)
Columba + one other, same location--a very interesting place, despite the cat pee smell
San Francisco Church
Magritte, the Terapeutist*
Notus, the South Wind* (FF!) + one other, same location
Ortus Depth*--*these three Paul's hinged stamps, elaborate & beautiful

I looked and looked for Playland Lives, to no avail. I thought I had the right circle of stones, but that was where it ended. No burned out log, nothing else fit just right. I was too tired to go driving to see if I hadn't gone far enough, and wouldn't claim the box missing, since I'm not sure I was in exactly the right spot. Just couldn't tell where 0 was supposed to be taken from...sometimes I get so confused!

Gotta go catch some winks now, it was a long day!

aT
