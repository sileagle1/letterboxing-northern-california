From: (Sierra64@aol.com) | Date: 2005-01-05 00:08:04 UTC-05:00
Yes! The April 30th Old Sacramento Gathering is on.

I scouted for spots in Old Sac last Friday, and found just the spot. It's a
grassy spot in Old Sac.. It's bordered by "I" and "J" Streets, Front Street,
and Commonwealth Alley. There are a couple of picnic tables there to use. If
it rains there are some picnic tables just across Front Street under a roof.
So we'll be good to go with sun or rain!

Hope everybody is carving up stamps and ready to place them. If we need a
meeting, I will be in that area on the weekend of Jan 15th, we could meet at
the site if needed.

I'll email to everybody that agreed to assist so we can plan it out.

Sierra Sally
