I managed to get the first of the Greek Wind deities done in time
for the weekend. This box is Notus, the South Wind, harbinger of
rain and fog, and son of Eos, the goddess of the dawn. There will be
more winged wind gods scattered around the city.

'Notus' marks my 25th planted letterbox! HOORAY!!!

I wanted to return to LbNCA letterboxing with a big splash, so I
worked hard to get three new boxes done this week. I'll also be
replacing the previously geotrashed "Nepenthe' soon and putting it
in a somewhat different spot, away from the neighboring geocache.

'Notus, the South Wind' has a large hinged folding stamp, as
does 'Star-Crossed' (retiring 8/15), 'Ortus Depths', and 'Magritte:
The Therapeutist'.

Get blown away!
