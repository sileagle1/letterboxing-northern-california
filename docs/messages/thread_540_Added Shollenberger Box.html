Added Shollenberger Box

From: moonrabbit2 (moonrabbit2@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-02-15 00:10:43 UTC

Another box has been added to Shollenberger Park, Petaluma as of
today. It's a 2-sided stamp, too. Although the bird migrations
haven't really started yet, there's certainly a lot of wildlife energy
going on between winter storms!

When planting today, there were 3 pairs of Tundra Swans, two of whom
decided to take to flight right near me--what a wooshing sound! And a
peregrine falcon showed up, majorly upsetting the many birds trying to
eat a bit before the rains started. Quite a lot of excitement in the
wetlands this morning!

The new box is now listed on Atlas Quest. It'll be updated on LBNA as
soon as I can figure out why my password suddenly doesn't work!
Hopefully, this evening or tomorrow.
