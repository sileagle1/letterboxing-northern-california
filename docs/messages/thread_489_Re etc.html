Hi to all our LB pals from up here in the Great White North!

To add our 2 cents worth - we found our first box on 3 Aug 03 (Don
and Gwen's Dam Raccoon in Oroville) and picked up F100 at Chowder's
Marina Pacifica Plumbing problem in Long Beach on 18 Jan 04.

All but 1 box were in the state of CA - the other was the fantastic
Incline Village box in NV, and were done, like Doublesaj, at a time
when there weren't a whole lotta boxes up in the northern part of
the state.

We're amused to see the difference from Right to Left Coast - Wanda
and Pete picked up 130 boxes in 4 DAYS in CT - and we picked up 54
boxes in 3 days in NY!

Now, up here in ON it's a different story - might take a while to
get F100 here as there are currently only 67 boxes in the province
and most of those are under 3 feet of snow (the others are under
only 2 feet lol!) We've planted a few, with a bunch more ready to go
in the spring.

We'll go back to lurking now!!
