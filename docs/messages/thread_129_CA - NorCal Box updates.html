Hi all!

Just wanted to updatethe status of a few boxes here in the north
central area!

Sutter Buttes - alive and well - no visitors since early April.

Duck in the Park - another easily accesible box awaiting some more
traffic!

Penni's Lincoln - nice little, easily accessible park! We were first
finders!

Apple Hill - super spot in the sierra foothills! First- finders for
this box, after an apparently loooong winter's nap! We also picked
up the Scar Paw HH, which has been healing since last June!

Empire Mine - we stopped by on our way home to update the book with
our personalized stamp (we used a store-bought stamp last Aug). This
box has been around since 2001 and has een a ton of visitors! The
book is full, so we put a new one in.

Hope to get down to Jackson next!
