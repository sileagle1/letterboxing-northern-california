From: Carole Hada (akacejay@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-08-22 14:03:54 UTC
In looking over the list, condiments are missing.....ketchup (catsup),
mustard, relish, onions, etc. Perhaps someone that hasn't signed up
to bring something as of yet could consider these items.

Personally, I don't think anyone should have to bring "grillables" for
everyone. It would work much better if each of us brought our own
hotdogs/hamburgers or "grillable" of choice and the buns, then a dish
to share or other shareable items.

For 1, 2 or even 3 people to have to bring the meat for all, plus what
they have already volunteered to bring seems to me to be a little
excessive.

Carole of DKK
