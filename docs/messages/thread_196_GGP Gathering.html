Based upon the favorable feedback from those who took the "Hunt for the Temple of Neptune of the Lost City of Atlantis", the box will be staying in its underwater location. There may be a very tiny adjustment to one of the 2 "key" boxes (to better hide it), but it won't affect the clues at all. I published an online web version yesterday. Here's its link: http://home.earthlink.net/~pgonyea/atlantis.htm

I really enjoyed myself at the gathering (although all the exchanges made my head spin), and it was wonderful having faces to put with the trail names.

I'm now ready to let my "find" count to finally be greater than my "plant" count... but you can expect more SF boxes from me, and as quirky as you'd expect!

Take care everyone - hope to see you again soon!
