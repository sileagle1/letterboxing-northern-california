I thought I'd provide you with some Paul-in-SF-style insanity while
you're waiting patiently for 'The Minotaur's Maze' (you can hear the
minotaur snorting and stomping his hooves in the background).

Here's a box that's been in secret progress for a while. It's
called 'Cat & Mouse', and features a cat named (of all things), Ortus.
There's some sort of pet/owner relationship between Ortus, and The
Therapeutist... but who the heck can figure it out. The 2 boxes are in
the same general area, but NOT down in the depths.

Here's the link: http://home.earthlink.net/~pgonyea/cat%20and%
20mouse.htm

And now, here starts the race -- Anna & the Kids versus Princess Lea!
Who will be the first finder? Place your bets now... (actually, I'm
betting on Ortus)...

Paul in SF
