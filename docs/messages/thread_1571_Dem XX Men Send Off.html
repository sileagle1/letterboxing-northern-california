From: indigoanigirl (cb.else@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-07-17 01:38:40 UTC
What?! Dem XX Men are leaving town! That's right, Dorks Anonymous is
relocating our outpost to Portland. Silicon Valley has far more
geeks than dorks.

Where: Buffalo Bills Brew Pub
1082 B ST.
Hayward, CA 94541

When: Sunday, July 22nd (thats this week) at 2 pm

Please send a shout out if you are definitely going to be there, or if
you think you might, so I can get a general head count for the nice
folks setting up tables.

There is parking in the rear behind Buffalo Bills, with a couple of
handicap spaces, but then you have to walk around to the front of the
building anyhow.

We'd love to meet up with you all for one last time before we hit the
road. (Feel free to bring cooties and hitchhikers that you want moved
up the coast).

See you there!
Cara & Hunter
