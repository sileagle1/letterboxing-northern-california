Gold Rush Romp--FUN!!

From: doublesaj (Doublesaj@aol.com) | Date: 2005-05-01 03:10:46 UTC
What a WONDERFUL event we had in Old Scaramento today! About 50 Newbie
to Avid (to obsessive) letterboxers gathered in perfect weather to find
the 27 boxes hidden for this event. Because this area is about 3 by 5
blocks, we were able to do it all on foot and most of us increased our
F-counts by a dozen or two!

Thanks to Sierra Sally for organizing this event to the gnat's eyelash
with great prizes and homemade goodies--including ice cream--for
dessert. Da Kool Kats donated a ton of prizes for lucky winners such
as: ArtTrekker who won prize baskets for her expertly carved stamps and
for the most planted for the event (7). Amanda from Seattle who won a
prize for all-time most boxes (somewhere around 1200) and she and
Princess Lea won for most boxes gathered today (27).

Thanks to everyone who stamped our books and who otherwise made this
event one to remember. Congratulations to you all!

~~DoubleSaj & Old Blue~~
