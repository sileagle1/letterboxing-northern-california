Bay Area Boxing

From: Lea Shangraw Fox (princesslea@alamedanet.net) | Date: 2004-03-25 20:15:58 UTC
Amanda was in the Bay Area yesterday so we got in a good day of
boxing. First we headed up to Marin and got Letterboxing Fools
2 "Miwok Trail" boxes - I couldn't believe that we were the first
finders since the boxes have been there since November. We also made
it to their Say Cheese! box which had had a bit of traffic. These
two can easily be done together as Novato Blvd. connects them.

Next we did box maintenance on Oompa Loompa which is alive and well,
and have only one mosquito bite to show for my efforts - they were
definitely swarming!

Then we had to pick up SleepyCat and headed to Berkeley where we did
box maintenance on Haunted Carousel and Pony Rides (inthe Tilden Pakr
series) and found the Little Fish in a Big Pond box. Nice succint
clues which we were happy to interpret correctly.

The biggest news is that Amanda found her 800th box! Woo Hoo!
Pictures were taken to commemorate the event.
