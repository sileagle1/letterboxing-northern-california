While Zairabear and I were at the 99c Only Store this afternoon we
came across a new (to us at least) Lock 'n' Lock clone called Maxi
Lock. Has anyone used/seen/tried these boxes? We love LnLs, but find
them cost prohibitive at times.

We just did a simple water tight test and we give it a B. We closed
up three sheets of toilet paper in it then put it in the sink and
filled the sink with water. With the water running we flipped it,
pushed it under water and basically toyed with it. After drying the
outside of the box we opened it to find the TP 85% dry. It seemed a
drop or two got in the box but anything in its own baggie would surely
be fine.

-Team Aahz
