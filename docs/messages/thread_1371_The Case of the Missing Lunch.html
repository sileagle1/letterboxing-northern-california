Hi all,

Da Kool Kats were really having an "off day", today. We hurried to
get the box of dem xx men before they pulled it and ended up leaving
our wheeled blue ice chest behind. When we got to the bright lights
of Vacaville I had Gary pull over so I could make a sandwich and
discovered the missing ice chest and lunch. We drove back to the park
but it was gone. I hope it is in the care of one of our letterboxing
friends. Let us know if you have it.

Thanks to all for the wonderful stamps we managed to get. We have got
to go back another day for quite a few others that we missed.
