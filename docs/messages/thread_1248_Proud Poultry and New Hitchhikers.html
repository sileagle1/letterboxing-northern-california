Just read in our local paper that the location of our Proud Poultry
Mystery Box is to be closed early in 2007. If you've been thinking
about looking for it, better do so soon!

Several of our letterboxes have had to be retired recently, usually
due to erosion or other damage to their home sites. Rather than let
them languish in a box, we have decided to turn them into hitchhikers.
Hope you enjoy finding them here and there. Please help them to
travel.
