It would be a good idea if you would read Jay Drew's last post at
this site before you start at the beginning. While some of these
letterboxes may or may not be lost and forgotten, some have been
adopted and are still listed at LBNA. I believe Painted Caves may
still be alive in San Diego area, but I haven't confirmed it. It is
a nice hike if anyone from that area is interested. Most of the
California letterboxes that are listed are listed very early , so
you will be able to locate the listings quite easily. Dumster Diving?
