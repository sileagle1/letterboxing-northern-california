Hi All,
Just wanted to thank everybody who planted boxes in the Sonoma/Napa
counties wine country. Mutau Paul and I did our best to find as many
in 2 days as we could. In fact we were so driven to find boxes that
we didn't even get a sip of wine!

Here's the report.

Alive and Well as of 4/23 or 4/24/06

Brannan Duck by Don & Gwen
Suisun City by Grumpy Grinch
Napa Valley Crusher by Blue Rose Gang
Salute Vallejo by OBJuan
Zin Country by Princess Lea
River Walk by Moon Rabbit
A Word to the Wise by Mim
Birds Eye View by Forman
G-9 Summit by Chaotic Heart
You and Me by Wascally Wabbit
Say Cheese by ltrbxnfools
Round Barn by artreker (we saw an HUGE wild turkey there, too cool)
Paradis Ridge Oaks by Mim (what a awesome place)
Calistoga by Bonedoc
Hidden Bones by Bondoc

We were not able to find:

Angel Wine
Twine Country
Brine Country
Maggie
Rock Repose
Every Flavor Bean

This was my first time in Wine Country and we had such a good time
and wonderful weather.

Thanks
Sierra Sally
