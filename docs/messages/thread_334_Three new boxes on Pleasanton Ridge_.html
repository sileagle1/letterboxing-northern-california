Three new boxes on Pleasanton Ridge.

From: Canis latrans (c._latrans@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-11-07 03:47:12 UTC

The 6-box Pleasanton ridge series is now complete. With the
current public access to the trail from a single staging area the
entire series is a 15 mile roundrip. The clues are designed for
hikers---that is how we planted them--but you can probbably do it
on a mountain bike. Most of the trails are accessible to
mountain bikes. Box 3 is a short ways down a hiker only trail.

The last drinking water on the trail is in Augustin Bernal park
near trail marker 21. This means you need to be sure to bring
sufficient water if you are going for boxes 4 and especially 5 and
6.

This trail is steep in places. Maps are available at the park.


The clues are at the following URL:

http://lane.t.tripod.com/box13.html

Coyotl Clan
