This is Princess Lea reporting from the road from Quechee VT. I have
been here for about 10 days so if you have written to me directly and I
have not responded - that's why! Internet access has been limited at
best. I was here for my birthday and, naturally, when letterboxing!
Unfortunately it has been raining EVERY DAY so there have been many
frustrating days when I should have been out on the trails. I spent
some time in Tolland County CT where there are so many boxes you
practically trip over them - definitely the center of the LB universe.

Welcome to the list to all the new folks - sounds like Grinch & Grumpy
are off to a running start! The San Jose Egypt Game boxes have not
been replanted yet. Last time I checked there were 2 large bulldozers
on the site, so renovations are still continuing. I'll let you know on
the clues page when they are ready.

- Princess Lea
