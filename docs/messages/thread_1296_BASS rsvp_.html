I would like to warn the organizers that the RSVP on Atlas Quest is not necessarily an
accurate headcount of who will actually show up at the event. Every event I have ever been
to has many people attending who never RSVPed (may have decided at the last minute, or
is one of those people who just likes to "surprise" everyone), and then some people who
said they were coming don't show up. Of course you have a ballpark idea of how many
people are coming, but it is not an exact science!
