Sea to Shining Sea

From: Michelle (bootzoocrew@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-03-27 04:07:17 UTC

Hi everyone, this post is a little different. It's for both
Letterboxers and Postal Letterboxers. About a week ago I was scrolling
thru the LB -announce post when I stumbled on the Sea to Shining Sea
Mystery box. You need to go to LB.org to read the instructions. To
make a long story short.. The launcher was taking ( I am not sure if
the quota is filled yet) a person from a list of States. If you
replied quick enough and were picked you get the box. I did and was
picked. #1 on the list. I just received it. What a beautiful stamp.
NOw the kicker is.. You take the stamp and of course do the normal PLB
part at home but you also take the stamp and the log book that is
provided with you and go letterboxing using their stamp also along with
yours and do the normal letterboxing part. This way not only do the
Launchers have your stamp but others from our states. I am going to
try and do a few tomorrow since we are kicking back here at home.
Sound like this is going to be really neat, and I just wanted to share
with you. Happy Easter.. Michelle
