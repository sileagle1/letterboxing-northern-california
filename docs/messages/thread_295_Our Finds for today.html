From: deiselluver (deiselluver@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-25 03:19:09 UTC


Hi everyone. Well Jeff and I went out on our first LB excursion.
WE set out with 6 Letterboxes to do. Due to some unusual
curcumstances when heading to SF to do 4 of them we ended up only
doing Columba. Wow what a great Hybrid this one was. Our thanks to
bthomas. So we then decided to skip the rest of the SF ones and head
out to Alien is Lost. I have been wanting to do this one since we
found out about LBing. WE were able to find box 2 & 3 but #1 seems to
be MIA! :( After this long journey we decided to try for Oompa
Loompa but by the time we hit Peteluma it was getting dark. So next
time.Maybe when we head out to Healdsburg Quartet. Sounds like fun.
Our thanks to Alien Explorers for a great afternoon.
