Amanda, Lea, Old Blue, DoubleSaj & I met up this morning for Lea's tour
of the Point Reyes boxes. We ended up getting 9 out of the 8 boxes we
went for. Yes, I wrote that right. We found all the boxes of a
mystery series somewhere in Marin county, #1-3 of the Alien is Lost by
the Alien Explorers, & #2, 3 & 4 of Blackbird's Tomales Point series.

getting 9. I'll be counting Tomales Point #4 as a RF1, refind count.

Thanks for putting it together Lea. It was the 1st time I'd had a
chance to out hunting as a group that wasn't at a gather, & had a great
time except for the soggy feet. I'll be hopefully posting some of the
pictures i took.
