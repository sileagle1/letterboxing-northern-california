I found the Arisacat micro-box at the Rutherford winery last week.
Unfortunately, the tiny logbook was damp & most of the stamp inks had
run. The Blue Family recorded finding it damp in Feb, so hopefully it
won't mildew. The stamp is a great, the view is incredible & my wife
tells me the wine is great, so I'd recommend it. Take the wine cave
tour too, very interesting. Maybe someone nearby could take this poor
stray in out of the rain & dry it out by the fire.

I also was able to pick up the Neighborhood school box in Dixon. Easy
20 minute detour for anyone going through Dixon during they day. The
box planter (the school principal I think) was very nice & glad to meet
another boxer. I was only the 2nd person to get the stamp & I think
it's been around since October.
