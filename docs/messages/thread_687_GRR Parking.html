We noticed a small parking lot in the alley at L St. and Front St.
It is $10.00 for 10 hours. A little less for fewer hours. If for
some reason you need to have access to your car, it may be easier from
this little parking area rather than using the parking garages and it
is a tad less $$$. You'd have to be one of the early ones to get
these spaces. Otherwise, the parking garage is probably better than
feeding the meters all day.
