We were first finders of Ty's "Grinding Stone" box near Winters
today. What a beautiful day for a hike! Coincidentally, the local
newspaper had a story about beautiful trails to visit and THIS was
one of them so there were MANY people on this well-manicured, native
plant-infested trail. (Mimulus, you'd love it!)

We were so inspired, we planted 2 boxes ourselves: one about 3 miles
from this one and one about 12 miles west, near Davis. So take a trip
up and enjoy them all!
