Can geocachers read?

From: jceg5540 (greendoc@sonic.net) | Date: 2004-10-14 15:18:32 UTC

Thanks to the heads up from Grumpy Grinch I found out a stamp was
removed by a neophyte geocacher. It turns out there is a geocache
very nearby and a beginner confused my box with a geocache. Despite
it being clearly labeled "This is not a geocahce, please do not remove
stamp" they signed the book, took the stamp and left a pikachu eraser.

I label all my boxes, but it failed to help in this case. Anyway, I
emailed them (My partner is a member of geocache.com so I could easily
contact them) and hopefully a geocacher will get converted to
letterboxing.

Anyway, it seems letterboxing is not prominently mentioned on their
website...perhaps as an organiztion LBers can ask them to include
mention of letterboxes, how to know the difference and how to respect
them.

Mimulus
