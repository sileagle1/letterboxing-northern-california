Hi all!

Clan Jiggs has been doing the whirlwind tour of the NorCal area
while my folks are visiting from Ontario. After suitably introducing
them to the LB world, we've been able to pick up and check up on a
few boxes in the area -

SUTTER BUTTES - Yuba City - alive and well, with a bonus still
waiting!

BRIDGEPORT - Penn Valley - alive and well (in a gem of an old time
swimmin' hole area).

GRANDMA'S HOUSE - Oroville - alive and well (NO visitors since last
October - this is a DRIVE BY Box with only 4 visitors!)

SHASTA SHADE - Dunsmuir - really nice box in a really nice park!

HEDGE CREEK FALLS - Dunsmuir - Wow! Super spot after a potentially
treacherous descent!

COCKTAIL CHARLIE - Sonoma - Any Jack London afficionados out there!
Great, short hike to a seldom-seen spot!

Still haven't picked up that Rocky Traiul box in Davis ...grrrr!
