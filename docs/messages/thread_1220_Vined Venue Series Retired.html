Vined Venue Series Retired

From: grumpygrinchy (ffuselier@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-06-20 22:32:00 UTC
The Vined Venue Series of Letterboxes in Ripon is retired as of 20
June 2006. The winter rains changed many of the landmarks. The vines
are overgrown to the point of obscuring some trails. And the
mosquitos are incredibly bothersome. Two boxes were missing, one was
unobtainable, and two we removed. They will probably go searching for
you as hitchhikers in the future.
