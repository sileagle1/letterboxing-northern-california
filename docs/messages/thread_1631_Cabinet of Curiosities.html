Mims,

Seeing as how I'm now retired (on disability), I have all the time in
the world... But I still have to juggle my 1,487 projects & interests.
It's easier now.

The "Cabinet" has been a major breakthrough for me, because it's
solved the problem of having so many boxes go missing in very short of
time. As much as I tried to think of the boxes as ephemeral (like
sunsets & flowers), it was ultimately discouraging.

The Curiosities are a win/win situation for both me and the hosting
stores -- the store's owners love the idea & it's one way of
recognizing the little business owner in a fun, creative way.

There will NEVER be one of my boxes in WalMart!

Let me know when you come to town. There are great little places for
lunch smack dab in the middle of the Cabinet!
