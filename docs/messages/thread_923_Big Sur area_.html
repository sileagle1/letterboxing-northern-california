Lisascenic -

We boxed that area last Feb - Don's suggestion of Way Cool is so true! That is an awesome box! There is a hybrid box called Saddle Rock there - great locale - but we didn't know if was a hybrid when we went - so it is a giant box! Also in that park is Canyon Owl and that is a fun little hike.

In Big Sur itself we got The Lorax and Amanda's Homestead and Oak Grove. We DID see a mountain lion on the trail coming back from Oak Grove. Fortunately, we didn't appear very tasty!

The Big Sur Marathon is another great box that Don mentioned - do your homeowrk on that one before you go - I think both Don and Gwen and The Boxalots found that one with only half the clues - a lucky find for us! Don't count on the folks in the area to be able to answer the questions.

And all the Monterery and Carmel area boxes are great finds too! Hope that helps - if you want to know more, email me!

Take care, Honour of The Boxalots

Hi Lisa,

I suggest that you leave enough time on the way down to Big Sur or on
the way back to stop in Monterey for the Wharf Cat Letterbox by Jay

far, but haven't been to any of the ones below Carmel. Every stamp in
the Monterey area has been hand carved, save one and they are all
excellent. I've heard that the Big Sur boxes are the same.
Excellent, that is!
