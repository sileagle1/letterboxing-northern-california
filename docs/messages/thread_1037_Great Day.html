Great Day

From: Wascally Wabbit (csmonty13@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-01-16 21:30:41 UTC
While kids at Gma's and my wife not wanting to go, I headed out and
got aT Wine Goddess-great stamp and cool hiding spot, attempted Big
Four, but couldnt find due to flooding/mudslides.... got "Calistoga"
and ran into a new boxer from Sebastopol-"Tinker" it was her 4th box.
then tried "a toast to the good times" and ran into "the box-a-lots"
and we searched, but couldn't find the box. So we all headed
to "missing bones" and after distracting 2 locals sitting right by the
box got that one. We soon parted and I headed up to and got "Angel
Wine". Didn't quite make it to "Aristacat" as the winery was closing.
Nice day, great drive, ipod on shuffle and loud, 2 exchanges, some
boxes...who could ask for more...
