Boxing afar

From: Hedglin, Nils A (Nils.A.Hedglin@Intel.Com) | Date: 2005-06-27 19:30:48 UTC-07:00
Took a quick trip through southern Idaho last weekend for family
visiting. Tried for 2 boxes, but both seemed missing (Emily's Decade &
Hockey Puck Out of Bounds). But, did get an exchange with A-Bear, an
extrememly helpful boxer who lives outside Boise. She sent me lists of
so many boxes along our route that it took 10 double sided pages in 8
point font to print out. If anyone's going between Boise & Idaho Falls,
let me know & I'll send you the list. It was frustrating to know we
were driving right passed so many boxes, but with a 2 yr old, you have
to drive as far as you can while she's asleep.
