Prissfaerie & I were sending messages via AtlasQuest when I mentioned
my upcoming PhotoShop mini-class. As she was unaware of it, I sent her
here to scroll through the messages for info. Then learning that was
unaware of this group, it made me think that there must be other newer
letterboxers in the region who also haven't joined LbNCA.

How can we alert these folks that our friendly and helpful LbNCA
exists? Is there any way to identify Northern California people who
are on AQ or LbNA/Lb.org that aren't LbNCA members?

The same thing happens with LbNY, too.

Paul

join the LbNCA group. About half of the people respond. As a moderator, I have a way of
sending invitations, so if anyone ever wants to me to send one to someone, just let me
know. Or do as Paul did and point them the direction of Yahoo Groups and the person can
sign up for themselves.
