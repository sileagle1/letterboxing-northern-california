From: Paul (pgonyea@earthlink.net) | Date: 2005-11-05 20:20:14 UTC
There! I graduated from the Greek Wind God Carving School now! Boreas
was my final project.

Boreas is in an extremely beautiful spot, well worth visiting. The
link is here:
http://www.atlasquest.com/lboxes/clue.html?boxId=10398

There are 4 more gods, including Skiron, who pours hot ashes out of a
jars, and Libbs, who holds the prow of a ship, but I think I can
safely move on to the many other boxes on my To-Do list.

This was a good idea, though, picking a theme of several related
complex images as carving practice. When I look at the 4 of them, I
can see definite improvements in my carving -- especially with drapery
folds. I'd recommend a project like this to anyone wanting to get
better at carving.

Paul in SF
