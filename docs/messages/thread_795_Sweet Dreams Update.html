Sweet Dreams Update

From: moonrabbit2 (moonrabbit2@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-09 21:53:34 UTC
I just completed a maintenance check on the Sweet Dreams Series in
Petaluma. All is well and in good shape. One box was exposed, but
all was there. At two of the boxes, the entire town of Petaluma
decided to show up so we ended up waiting around with binoculars for
5-10 minutes, looking like birders. Hikers moved on quickly, and we
were able to finish rehiding with no problems.

I did notice that in two spots, there had been significant park
maintenance done. Both boxes were fine. I suspect one box had to
have been discovered by the park crew, but the rock was neatly placed
over the box.
