From: ANTHONY ZUCCO (gadzuke@flash.net) | Date: 2005-09-01 23:32:23 UTC-07:00
Carole -

Thank you so much for the concern. My family is all fine and in a part of Louisiana that was out of the path. My brother informed me today that gas pumps were shut off in town and they are going to rationing in Shreveport. He reports that refugees (just never imagined using that word in conjunction with Americans in our own country) are in every available space - and my Mom has opened her home long-term to some folks from New Orleans. My only concern for my family at the moment is the fact that my Mom is traveling alone. She is in Atlanta right now and needs to get back to northern Louisiana in the next few days. She told me tonight that gas in Atlanta was at $4.95/gallon. She waited in line for hours and has a full tank - but wouldn't get home on that. And there is no gas in the majority of Mississippi. So - she will probably enjoy the hotel there in Georgia for awhile longer.

I guess I am still in shock from the complete devastation. If I remember correctly - the Grumpy Grinches have family in southern Louisiana....how are they? My husband is so busy with the huge response from the military....the task is enormous. I lurk on the LBLaMiss list - and we are still waiting to hear from some of the amazing boxers from the New Orleans area.

We did a small bit of boxing when we were home for a month - re-planted and planted a new one in Louisiana - got a couple of TX boxes....but truly LOVED Paul's stamps that we jumped on the day after we returned! We had a great boxing day in SF....hunted 7 and found 7! And were thrilled that Playland does indeed LIVE!

Carole - Thanks again for the concern - we are fine...it has just been a tough year. And like Mim...we are always up for a gathering. The road ralley idea courtesy of Double Saj and Old Blue did sound fun....there is an east coast event like that as well I think.

Take care, Honour of the Boxalots
