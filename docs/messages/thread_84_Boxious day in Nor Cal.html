Boxious day in Nor Cal

From: (Doublesaj@aol.com) | Date: 2004-04-03 22:30:47 UTC-05:00
We added 7 boxes to our F count and 1 HH today in this beautiful 75 degree
weather. This 'loop' of boxes we made is a good one, taking us from Yuba City,
up to Grass Valley then over to Folsom. We highly recommend it!! Here's a
status report:

Bok Kai--first finders
Ruff & Ready--a wedding in progress culminating with the honking of horns
as we got the second box was a real bonus to these cute stamps. For
anyone who watched the Ruff & Ready cartoons as a kid, you won't be
able to get that darned theme song out of your head!!
Bridgeport-- what an interesting place and a great, big stamp! This is the
longest covered bridge in the U.S.!!
(These stamps are all by the Jiggs Family and are really great boxes!)
Empire Mine--planted by Funhog in 2001, this is a great historic location for
a day of discovery and a lovely picnic.
Folsom "Castle Park" Series--in the over-built town of Folsom, a man-made
wetlands any birdlover would adore houses these 3 boxes beautifully
carved by a newbie, Diana. Don't miss these!!

Happy boxing!!
~~Doublesaj & Old Blue~~
