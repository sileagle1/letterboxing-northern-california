New box in Yolo County: "Cupid's Arrow"

From: doublesaj12 (Doublesaj@aol.com) | Date: 2004-02-15 17:54:30 UTC
For Valentine's Day 2004, we're on the lookout for that dear rosy
Cherub, the Prince of Love, Cupid. Cupid is all about love, we know
that. But hitting the target requires accuracy, aim, trajectory. That
means practice. You wouldn't want your sister falling goo-goo-eyed in
love with that weird guy next door! Find Cupid's Arrow and you'll
see, first-hand, where the little guy does his practicing. Who knows,
you just might spy him behind a tree! By the way, Cupid is never seen
here after dusk.

Go south on Mace Blvd. Leave the residential area, pass a Campbell
Soup sign, levee, bridge, about 4 miles all told. Watch for the Yolo
County Grassland Regional Park, "The Oasis" entrance. "No Broadheads,
No Crossbows" marks your path. Wend your way through this
interesting, shady place to target area number 8. Footbridge, east
side, underneath about 3 feet from the edge.
