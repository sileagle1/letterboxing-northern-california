Princess Lea has the right idea! You just have to get out there and
box! Yeah, it took a while to clean all the mud off my hiking boots
when we got home and I did do a little skate-n-slide along the way
(which entertained Old Blue, always at my side) but we ended up with
12 boxes and an HH in 2 days. Here's a list of what's alive and well:

Charming Chapeau and Sedate Sedan (by Grumpy Grinch) and Nancy Takes
Manhattan and Big Apple (by Amanda from Seattle) in Antioch, CA, The
Old Sacramento Cemetery by Chantt (in Sacramento, of course). The
rest, all in Lodi by Grumpy Grinch: Lodi Libation, Blushing
Butterfly, Pretty Posy, Downstream Drifter, Shy Squirrel, Little
Lamplight and Basic Bunny (love that alliteration!).

We managed to plant one too (making P 50 for us!), along with the
rest on the lovely hike in Antioch. If you can't find the clues to
all these boxes, don't forget to check that masterfully comprehensive
site, www.atlasquest.com ! In fact, you'll need to do so if you want
the clues to the box we planted!

Happy Boxing New Year to you all!!
