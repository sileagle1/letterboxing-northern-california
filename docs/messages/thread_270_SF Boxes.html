Hi All,

We recently spend a week in San Francisco during a spell of fabulous
weather. We were able to enjoy it while doing a bit of boxing. We
wished we had time for more; however here's the lowdown on our finds
and attempts.

FOUND:
Star-crossed- Amazing stamp!!
G3 Conservatory of Flowers 1 and 3- Both boxes in good shape.
Yes, Noe!- What a lovely street with creative clues.

ATTEMPTS:
Corousel's Pink Treat- We were sure we were in the right locations,
but no sign of the box. Maybe it was only there during the recent
gathering--?
G3 Conservatory of Flowers 2- Found the exact locale, but no box.
Playland Lives!- ditto

We'd love to know if anyone has info on our attempts. And thank you
to the planters for your efforts. We appreciated them during our
sojourn. :>

Shifteight
Eureka, CA
