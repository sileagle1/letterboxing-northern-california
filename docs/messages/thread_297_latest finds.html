Well, the snow is dumping down again today. It looks
like we've gotten a couple of feet out there! I'm so
glad I escaped yesterday afternoon to Folsom to look
for Diana's boxes ("Folsom" and "Folsom's Cohn Park").
The first series took me on a lovely walk past a marsh
replete with cattails and bird life. Her stamps are
great and so are the convenient places to sit and use
them.

The second box in the second series gave me my first
critter experience. It was a close encounter of the
formicid kind! Yikes! I relocated the box a couple
feet away, in hopes the ants would not follow, but
future finders beware!

Well, after all the press, I can't wait to get down to
the coast and check out Alien for myself. It's not in
the immediate future (especially if he is getting as
much rain as we are snow!), but you never know when
I'll just up and go.

SO looking forward to SS's haunted house!
