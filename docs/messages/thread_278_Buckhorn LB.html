Dear fellow LBers,

Just want everyone to know what a great letterbox you're missing.
Today we went in search of BUCKHORN planted by Funhog.
The stamp is exceptional and wait until you see the book cover!!
The trail is very nice, as well.. We even did it in the dark, although
I wouldn't
recommend doing it. Besides, you want to go so you can see the sights.
Just
before sunset would be ideal. I was constantly in fear of snakes, since
I couldn't see where we were going. We only had a pin light flash light
and there
were no trail maps in the holder for us to take along. So, as you're
walking the trail,
think of us doing it in the dark. You may not want to take the trail
it after a heavy rain,
so get to it now!!! It was last found by JIGGS exactly 11 months ago.

Da Kool Kats,
Gary and Carole
