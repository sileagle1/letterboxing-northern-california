From: paulonthepark (pgonyea@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-08-23 02:19:14 UTC
I was at a certain LbNCa gathering recently, when a certain fellow (a
frog with boots comes to mind) from one of those uncertain blueberry-
growing states up north, made a vague and uncertain remark about the
clues I write. I believe it was something to the effect that they
were a bit verbose or maybe it was just that the word count was
rather high.

So let me be succinct and make myself perfectly clear: I live alone
and have two cats, (Reno and Vegas), that are sick and tired of
listening to me ramble on. So someone has to suffer...

Getting to the point, let me not mince words, and I hereby announce
that I have planted a box and written clues for it with an extremely
low word count, simply in order to quiet the frogs and reduce my word
count average.

BUT, this box is a thing of beauty and well worth pursuing... It is
Star-Crossed
http://home.earthlink.net/~pgonyea/star-crossed.htm

Paul in SF
