From: grumpygrinchy (ffuselier@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-10-24 19:40:39 UTC
We had an amazing day of letterboxing yesterday. The highlight was
the rope descent to Da Kool Kats' Tribal Letterbox (Waterfall #4).
Not many people have ventured out for this treasure and we felt
privileged to find it.

We were disappointed by the rapidly deteriorating quality of our
stamp, so we spent this morning carving new ones. We have replaced
our small stamps for boxes as well as our personal stamp for
exchanges. You'll know the new ones by the hats the Grumpy Grinch now
wears.
