Natomas Oaks and Davis Fun

From: grumpygrinchy (ffuselier@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-08-18 05:45:29 UTC
Grumpy and I made our third try today to find the Natomas Oaks
Microbox. We finally located it and happily stamped our logbook.
The paper strip in the Natomas canister is now FULL!

We had such fun discovering 7 of Davis's letterboxes. We started
with Cupid's Arrow, then found The Best of Davis 1, 2, 3, and 5.
Either we turned over many wrong rocks at #4, or it has gone
missing. We enjoyed the Rocky Trail and were very pleased to find
its box - yes it does exist, Jiggs. We finished our day with Ryan's
clever box at UC Davis.

We feel that we got a great look at some of Davis's best offerings
while we walked and searched. Thanks to all who took time to
prepare and place their boxes.

Grinch (Grumpy's other half)
