a very successful saturday

From: nicknameredinsf (lauracam1976@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-03-06 23:06:30 UTC

thanks to da kool kats AND to Mimulus, Red, Scott and SFmomo had a
great day at Sugarloaf Ridge state park. We hit all 6 Sideways boxes,
all of the Planet Walk Boxes, Abamit and Her Shadow and Pomo
Hummingbird - for a grand total of 20 boxes. (20!! Our best to date
has been 7...but really, we have da kool kats to thanks for their
excellent help). We also picked up 3 HHs (TGIF HH #1, Ant M, and The
African Traveler). We dropped someone off too.

About Pomo Hummingbird...we found the box open and the journal
completely soaked (it was sitting in water in the box). It looked like
the work of an animal. We spent some time squeezing all of the water
we could out of the journal (the stamp was okay). The journal is
salvageable and it looked like most stamp images were still intact. We
put everything back as dry as we could get it.

thanks!
