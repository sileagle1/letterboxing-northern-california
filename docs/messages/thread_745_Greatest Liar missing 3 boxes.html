Re: Greatest Liar missing 3 boxes

From: Inbal (inbalit@mindspring.com) | Date: 2005-08-23 05:31:24 UTC
Here's an update on the Greatest Liar series.

Boxes 1, 2 and 3 are still there. If anyone went there and didn't
find them, I'm not sure how to explain that - they're definitely
there.

Meanwhile, a letterboxing family did their daughter's 10th
birthday on a letterboxing theme and replaced boxes 4, 5 and 6
with temporary boxes. If anyone wants to find all 6 boxes, they
are there as of 2 days ago!

Box 5 is in a slightly different location than the original, but if you
look all along the logs you'll find it under some pieces of bark, on
the side of the logs away from the path.

Thanks to those who came by already!

By way of a more personal update, our son is less intensely into
letterboxing, and I've been dealing with a health problem for
going on 3 months, which explains why you have not heard from
us all this time. We've boxed a little though, and hope to do more
some time soon.
