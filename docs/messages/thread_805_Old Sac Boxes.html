Lee and Nancy,

I have been checking your boxes regularly and I will almost bet the
Theater box and Pony Express boxes are still there. If any are
missing, it would be the RR Museum box. I'm surprised that it has
lasted as long as it has. Almost every week-end the caboose is rented
out for birthday parties and they (the children) leave very little
untouched. Also, during the week schools send children to Old Sac on
field trips and the caboose and theater are always bombarded with
busy, inquisitive children.

I will also check on your boxes, possibly this week, because I know
exactly where they are.
