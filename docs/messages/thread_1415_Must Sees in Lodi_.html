From: Hedglin, Nils A (Nils.A.Hedglin@Intel.Com) | Date: 2006-12-29 09:22:15 UTC-08:00
Not sure which of these are still around, but Alien Smorgasborg is a
must & I think in the general Lodi area (maybe between Lodi &
Stockton). & you have to go in to the store to understand the stamp.
Spencer's Bone is a nice walk in the woods, but I'm pretty sure that's
where I got poison oak, probably not a problem now. Calaveras River
Trail is a nice walk along the river. Tiger Tale nice walk on UOP
campus. Last 2 are in or around Stockton. I there's one other in Lodi,
but you have to figure that one out yourself.

I, oh, silly me, assumed you may be coming over this way for

Perhaps I need to clarify. Any must haves in Lodi are no longer
there, except for Lodi Libations. Without looking, I think you got
the Lodi Libations, which by the way, is a very nice stamp, the last
time you were over this way. Tiger Tale is quite nice, as well. We
first tried for this one in the dark; no surprise there, and had to go
back during the day.

If you come over through the Delta, you might try to get the one by
Don and Gwen. We haven't made it to this one, but I'm sure it will be
a great stamp considering who planted it.

Once again, not much left in the Stockton/Lodi area. Enjoy the drive.
