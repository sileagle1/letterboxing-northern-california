Vertigo, my 1st and classic letterbox, is alive again!

From: Paul (pgonyea@earthlink.net) | Date: 2005-08-24 22:20:29 UTC
Much like Madeleine in Hitchcock's masterpiece, 'Vertigo', the Vertigo
#1 letterbox has reappeared!

For those of you unfamilar with this particular letterbox, here are
some fun facts to know and share:

- this was my first planted letterbox, and quite complex for a starter
- the clues were channeled to me by Hitchcock's spirit
- it will make you dizzy or feel faint
- 'Vertigo' was Hitch's 'valentine to San Francisc'
- there is a 'Vertigo #2', planted by Princess Lea
- this replant is in honor of Mimulus, as it's her favorite letterbox
- I've watched the movie at least 57 times
- it's much better disguised this time - hooray!

Clues: http://home.earthlink.net/~pgonyea/vertigo1.htm

So, rent, buy, or put 'Vertigo' as #1 in your Netflix queue, and get
busy!

Also, 'Notus, the South Wind' may have blown away -- but I haven't
confirmed it yet, so you may want to wait a bit before looking for it.

With acrophobic immediacy, yours truly,
Paul in SF
