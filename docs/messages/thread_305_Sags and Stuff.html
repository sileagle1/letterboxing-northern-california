Okay.... another topic....


> is it okay to scan and post the microbox visitor logs on our
> website?

I'd definitely have to urge you *not* to do this. There are some
people who'd rather not have their stamp image online, and unless you
get permission from the people that logged in, I wouldn't recommend it.

The letterboxing.org FAQ even has this to say: "Don't publish or
re-transmit other peoples' clues or stamp images without their
permission."

However, if you put a disclaimer in your clues telling people that by
stamping into the box they are giving consent to have their entry
scanned in and displayed on your website, I don't think it would be an
issue.

If you just want to provide a list of everyone who logged in--without
the scanned images, just a list of names and perhaps the date it was
found--I doubt anyone would be too upset with that either.
