Took a nice morning walk to find the Meyer's Ranch letterboxes in Union City. A great little
trail but sadly no boxes for us.

We found the remains of one in the creek. Box was in pieces and a few yards downstream
we found most of the logbook. Stamp was nowhere to be seen.

We didn't find the second one either - although I have to admit we didn't spend that much
time looking around for it.

I picked up and trashed out the remains of the first box - no sense in leaving bits of
plastic scattered in the creek. And we've got the logbook too. Lizardbutt's Mom - let me
know if you'd like me to mail the logbook to you. Or maybe I can try to figure a way to get
it to you at the SF gathering.

cheers,
