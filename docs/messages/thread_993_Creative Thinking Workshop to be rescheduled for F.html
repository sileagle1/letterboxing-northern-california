From: Paul (pgonyea@earthlink.net) | Date: 2005-12-23 12:41:13 UTC
Sorry, folks, but I need to reschedule... it turns out that work is
sending me to Phoenix that week... plus, January is a bit heavy duty
with work projects, and I may have to fly to Utica, NY as my very
dear 'adopted mom' from my extended family is extremely ill.

I'm considering the middle of February instead. How about SATURDAY,
FEB. 18th? I hope that works for most of you.

I have been pulling together some materials for the class, and I'm
very excited about the workshop. I'll get you thinking!

Also, last week I got together with Da Kool Kats who came into SF --
we arranged to meet and exchange at the folk art store that hosts 'Day
of the Dead'. We had a great little visit, then the Kats went on a
blitzkreig, nabbing several of my boxes, and one other as well for a
total of 7! They were FFs for my tiny 'If All Else Fails' box, which
has been lonely for over 3 weeks waiting for RedSF, Princess Lea, or
Anna & the kids.

I just carved a stamp for an event in NY called, 'Desperately Seeking
Sun', that I'm sending to the event organizer as part of a
Letterboxing Bingo game. (Object is to find event boxes that
correspond to a big bingo card... get a line stamped in, and win). The
stamp image is a woman lying on a beach under a parasol, reading.

Welcome back, Mim! The trip sounds like it was wonderful! Lisascenic
and Robb, still want a day in Oakland...

Paul in SF
