Hi all,
Met up with Team Safari (Mary & Dan) on Friday & had a nice dinner.
It sounded like Mary (trailname: Mary RI) has been letterboxing for at
least 4-5 years. She mentioned they got married 2 years ago, so I'm
guessing Dan hasn't been boxing as long. They don't keep track of their
statistics, but Dan said he tried counting once recently & gave up after
500 finds.

They were able to do the Fanny Ann's box with my help. Unfortunately,
the 2 final clues have been erased, so I'm going to mark the box as
inactive for now. They did leave a hitchhiker in the box, so I'll be
passing that on soon. I recognized CelticQuinn's name on the HH's cover
& they said it was originally a postal letterbox that they got
permission from CelticQuinn to turn into a HH.

They headed up to Tahoe on Sunday, so I suggested they send artTrekker
an email too.
