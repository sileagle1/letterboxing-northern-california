Hello All,

Wanted to make a final call for this great event in 2 weeks.
According to the AtlasQuest posting, it looks like we'll have around
50 people if everybody shows that has signed up. WHOOHOOO! Meet at
the picnic tables between I and J Streets at Front Street in Old
Sacramento at 10:00 am (or whenever you can get there) MANY boxes
will be planted in the immediate area within easy walking distance
for all ages.

Bring your own picnic or buy food from the miriad of shops and
vendors nearby. Don't forget your sunblock and hat!

We've had lots of ideas, all of which sound good to me, I think we'll
try all of them. If you want to be in charge of your idea, please
bring the supplies and implement your suggestion. I'll help in any
way I can the day of the gathering.

So far the following is the agenda.

Saturday, April 30th
10 am to 4 pm

10 AM to Noon Stamp Exchanges,
clues & maps distributed,
carving demo (informal)

Noon Bring your own picnic lunch
12:30 PM Photo

4 pm Awards,Door prizes and desserts
