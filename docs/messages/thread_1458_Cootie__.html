From: grumpygrinchy (ggboxer@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-02-10 06:03:25 UTC
Grumpy and I are now settled into our new home in San Jose and hope to
return to the letterboxing life again soon.

While we were moving in we used some of the plastic utensils, plates
and cups we had leftover from the two letterbox gatherings we attended
this past summer. Imagine our surprise when we lifted a plastic glass
to discover a letterbox cootie nestled underneath. It had been hidden
during the BASS in Oakland and remained undiscovered throughout the
Vacaville event.

We consider it a welcome reminder of some of our good boxing friends.
We had planned to take it to the Tucson gathering last week, but
illness kept us home. Who knows. Maybe it will reappear in San
Diego - or on April Fools Day! We warned; be alert; be stealthy. :-)
