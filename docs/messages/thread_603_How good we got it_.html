check out this old link to letterboxing.org, for some of us new
boxers it shows how few and far between boxes were around here(and
all over) just 2 years ago! Really cool and an eye opener. Makes ya
appreciate the very active planters in our area like Princess Lea,
Doublesaj and Old Blue, Funhog and Mimulus - and many others of
course!

retired?

From: tammierocks (tammierocks@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-03-17 16:57:01 UTC

Wow, in just a few years so much has happened. I just wanted to let
you know that Skunk cabbage is still around! It was actually my
first find less than a month ago. The Redcrest Fairyland was MIA,
but has been replaced (according to the clue), but I haven't made it
there yet; plan to soon!
I also want to say that by the summer, Humboldt will be rockin',
letterbox style, so plan a little vacation up this way to cool off.
Thanks to everyone for giving my family a chance to get out and do
something fun!!
familyT
