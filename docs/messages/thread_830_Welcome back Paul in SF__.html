Yes, I was so excited to see Paul's newly planted
boxes, that after work, I went right away and found
Ortus Depths. What a great location. No one was there
when I went, but people who go on a sunny day will
want to practice stealth.

I didn't have time to look for the other box, but I
might suggest that people look at a map when preparing
to look for the box because I know one can get to twin
peaks via Clarendon from both directions (roughly east
or west). There is a center divider most of the way,
but one can turn left onto Johnstone. Johnstone begins
at Clarendon.
