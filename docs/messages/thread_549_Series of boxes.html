The series I think Grumpy Grinch is referring to is the Fresno
County multicultural series by Sierra Sally. The little Jiggs' and I
did this series (all 11 in about 4 hours - and 100 or so miles of
driving!) We were first finders of the last 8 in the series. We
thoroughly recommend it to anyone, but I do know tha Sally had a
heck of a time keeping track of so many boxes! Might be food for
thought for anyone else thinking of putting together such an
ambitious project. Come to think of it, the California Missions (of
which we only got one or two before moving) falls in the same
category...anyone close to getting all of those? Just curious!
