Our two Lakeside Lovelies boxes, Blushing Butterfly and Pretty Posey
#2, are retired as of May 1, 2006. We temporarily removed them from
the Lodi Lake area during the rainy season. When we tried to return

not replace the boxes here. We will probably find a new use for them
in the future.
